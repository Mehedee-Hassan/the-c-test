Delco Business Associate signs sponsorship deal with Wedding Festival 2016
Festivity, organiser of ‘The Westin Presents -Wedding Festival 2016’, has recently signed platinum sponsorship deal with Delco Business



Associate Ltd, a sister concern of Hamid Group.

The 2-day wedding festival will be held at the Westin in Dhaka from October 21, 2016.
