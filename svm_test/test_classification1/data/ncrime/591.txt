UK minister Alok Sharma meets Khaleda
United Kingdom minister for Asia Pacific Alok Sharma on Friday met with the Bangladesh Nationalist Party chairperson, Khaleda Zia, at her Gulshan office in the capital.

After concluding the meeting party’s secretary general Mirza Fakhrul Islam Alamgir, who was also present at the meeting told reporters that they discussed on the health, trade, education and other issues of the countries.

Fakhrul quoting Alok Sharma said UK always wants to see free and fair general elections in Bangladesh.

Khaleda seek contribution of United Kingdom in country’s development and sought measures to extend and maintain relationship between the countries, he said.

British High Commissioner to Dhaka Alison Blake was also present during the meeting.
