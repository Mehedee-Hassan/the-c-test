Cadet College Club celebrating 14th founding anniv
Cadet College Club is celebrating its 14th founding anniversary in Dhaka by organising weeklong sporting competitions.

Cultural Night, the main event of the celebrations, will be held on April 14, a press release stated.

The club arranged different game completions, namely table tennis, badminton, carom and chess for the members from March 27. Swimming completion was held on April 1 for the members and their children.

IFIC bank sponsored annual feast for the club members on April 6 while Kashem group sponsored Golf tournament held on April 7.
