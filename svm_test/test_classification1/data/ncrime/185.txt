Samadhara poetry festival held
A poetry festival organised by literary magazine Samadhara was held at the National Theatre Hall of Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy on Thursday.

Around 200 noted and upcoming poets read out their poems and recitation artistes took part at the festival titled Islami Bank Samadhara Kabita Utsab 2017.

Cultural affairs minister Asaduzzaman Noor was present at the festival as chief guest. Poets Nurul Huda, Helal Hafiz, Nirmalendu Goon, vice-chairman of Islami Bank Ahsanul Alam, editor of Samidhara Salek Nasir Uddin, among others also attended the inauguration session.

Poet Nirmalendu Goon was awarded with Samadhara Sahitya Puraskar 2017 at the progrmame.

In his speech, Nirmalendu Goon thanked Samadhara for the award. ’The honour will always inspire me to write better,’ said Goon.

This is for the third time Samadhara has organised such festival. Last year, Helal Hafiz received Samadhara Sahitya Puraskar.

The discussion session was followed by presentation of poems of Rabindranath Tagore, Kazi Nazrul Islam, Jibananda Das by noted and upcoming recitation artistes.
