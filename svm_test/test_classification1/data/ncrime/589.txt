Bangladesh recognizes republic of Kosova
Bangladesh recognized the Republic of Kosova as an independent and sovereign country on Monday.

The cabinet this afternoon endorsed a proposal to recognize the Republic of Kosova as independent and sovereign state.

The Foreign Ministry places the proposal in the weekly cabinet meeting chaired by prime minister Sheikh Hasina, said cabinet secretary Mohammad Shafiul Alam.

Along with Bangladesh a total of 114 countries recognized Kosova so far.
