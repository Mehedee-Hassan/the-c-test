AL cancels reception for Hasina
Awami League has cancelled a reception programme for prime minister Sheikh Hasina on her return home from India today, according to a party statement issued on Sunday evening.

The party cancelled the reception for the prime minister as per her direction, said the statement signed by AL office secretary Abdus Sobhan Golap.

Hasina is set to return to Bangladesh today after her four-day state visit to India.

Awami League and its alliance had earlier announced a grand reception for Hasina on behalf of the party, the alliance and the nation.
