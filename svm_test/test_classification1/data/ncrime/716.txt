Khaleda’s news conference today
Khaleda Zia. --New Age file photo

Bangladesh Nationalist Party chairperson Khaleda Zia would address a news conference on Wednesday to inform her party’s stance over deals and memorandums of understanding signed with India during prime minister Sheikh Hasina’s visit to New Delhi.

The news conference would be held at the BNP chairperson’s Gulshan office at 4:30pm, her press wing member Shamsuddin Didar told New Age Tuesday evening.
