The best of Baishakh
Fashion Entrepreneurs Association of Bangladesh (FEAB) organised a spectacular fashion show, followed by a concert, at the Art Gallery Plaza of Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy on April 9. The leading fashion houses presented their Baishakhi collection at the gala event, promoting the local fashion industry, whilst Joler Gaan serenaded the packed crowd with their melodious tunes. Azra Mahmood was the choreographer of the fashion show.
