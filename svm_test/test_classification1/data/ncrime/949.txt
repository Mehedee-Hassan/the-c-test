Threads of Heritage
On June 30, 2016 a fashion show was organised by Her Excellency, Saida Muna Tasneem, the Bangladesh Ambassador to Thailand, at the Queen Siriket Centre, Bangkok. Renowned Bangladeshi designer Maheen Khan presented her signature Maysir designs at the event. Three other fashion designers - Rina Latif of Bangladesh, Sudjitt Sudjitt of Finale Wedding Studio, Thailand and Yukimi Kawashima of Japan also showcased their creations at the show.
