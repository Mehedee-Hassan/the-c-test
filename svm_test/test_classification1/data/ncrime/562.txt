Fire at Karwan Bazar TCB building put out
A fire broke out at Trading Corporation of Bangladesh building in the city’s Karwan Bazar area on Monday morning and it was extinguished within 30 minutes.

Fire service sources said the fire broke out from the ground floor of the building around 7:40am.

On information, two firefighting units from the Fire Service and Civil Defence headquarters rushed in and put out it at 8:10am, said a duty officer at the fire station.

It is still not clear what caused the fire.
