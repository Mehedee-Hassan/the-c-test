BNP leader Dolon held
Kazi Mazharul Islam Dolon

Police arrested Kazi Mazharul Islam Dolon, a central executive committee member of Bangladesh Nationalist Party and also leader its physicians wing Doctors Association of Bangladesh, at a house of Dhanmondi in Dhaka on Sunday night.

Officer-in-Charge of Kalabagan Police Station Yasir Arafat said that police only carried out a court order issued earlier asking to arrest Dolon in an anti-terrorism case filed with Tejgaon police station in 2015.

He said he was arrested from the house no-39 of Dhanmondi 4/A area last night.

Details will be informed later, the OC added.

The BNP secretary general, Mirza Fakhrul Islam Alamgir, in a statement condemned the arrest of Dolon.
