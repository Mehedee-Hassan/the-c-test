Child drowns in Habiganj
A child drowned in a pond at Pikepara village in sadar upazila in Habiganj.

The child was identified as Soyrob, 5, son of Kismot Ali and a class one student of a local Kindergarten school.

Locals said Soyrob fell into the pond when he was playing beside it near his home.

People rescued Soyrob and sent him to Habiganj Adhunik Sadar Hospital where the duty doctors declared him dead.

Officer-in-charge of Habiganj police Md Easinul Haque confirmed the incident.
