Mehedi replaces Hogg in CPL
Bangladesh’s Mehedi Hasan was included Trinbago Knight Riders squad for the upcoming edition of Caribbean Premier League, the tournament authorities announced on Tuesday.

Mehedi came to the side as a replacement of Australia’s Brad Hogg, who declared himself unavailable

Mehedi, who will be making his tournament bow, will join Sakib Al Hasan as second Bangladeshi for the upcoming edition of the tournament.

Sakib will play for Jamaica Tallawahs in the tournament.

The next edition of CPL will begin on June 29 with the final scheduled on August 7.
