Family Planning’s central warehouse catches fire
A fire broke out at the central warehouse of the Directorate General of Family Planning at Mohakhali in Dhaka early Sunday.

The fire erupted in the tin-shed warehouse around 12:15am, said Mahmud, an inspector at the control room of Fire Service and Civil Defence.

Fourteen units of firefighters rushed in around 12:40am and put out the blaze around 3:00am with the help of locals, Mahmud added.

However, it is still not clear what caused the fire.
