Udichi’s three-day people’s song festival begins
Artistes of Udichi Shilpi Goshthi perform after inauguration of Satyen Sen Ganasangeet Utsab o Jatiya Ganasangeet Pratijogita at Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy on Tuesday.— Snigdha Zaman

Cultural organisation Udichi Shilpi Goshthi’s three-day people’s song festival began at Bangladesh Shilpakala Acsdemy premise on Tuesday afternoon.

Udichi has been organising the festival cum competition titled Satyen Sen Ganasangeet Utsab o Jatiya Ganasangeet Pratijogita annually since 2010 marking birth anniversary of noted author-politician Satyen Sen (March 28, 1907- December 05, 1985).

The festival includes cultural show, discussions, seminar and people’s song competition in which artistes from all the division of the country are participating.

Singer Rathindranath Roy inaugurated the festival as chief guest in presence of cultural personalities Syed Hasan Imam and Kamal Lohani, noted people’s song singers from India Amitabh Mukhopadhyay and Purabi Mukhopadhyay, among others.

On that programme an album featuring 11 songs of Satyen Sen was released.

Udichi artistes performed at the cultural show on that evening. They presented a lyrical drama titled Amar Larai Sabai Oikataan on that evening. Compiled and directed by Sangeeta Imam, the production protested against the government’s plan to set up a power plant near Sundarban in Rampal.

The festival will also feature a seminar on current status people’s song in the country at 4:00pm on Wednesday. Journalist Dipankar Goutam will talk at the seminar. The day’s programme will also feature presentation of Udichi’s district-level branches.

The concluding day’s programme will be held at the Shawkat Osman Memorial Auditorium of the Public Library.

Purabi Mukhopadhyay, Amitabh Mukhopadhyay and Rathindranath Roy will perform on that evening.
