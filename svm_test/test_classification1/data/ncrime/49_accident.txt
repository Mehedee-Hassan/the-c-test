Vehicle crashes into US gas pump creating huge ball of flames
A surveillance video shows a gas pump burst into a ball of flames after the driver of sport utility vehicle drives through the place.

It even did not end there, after surviving the flames the SUV hit a tree and split in half, critically injuring one person inside, KGO-TV, an ABC owned-and-operated television station, reports quoting police in Seattle of US.

The surveillance video was captured the scene Monday (US time) night, it said.

Police said the SUV was racing away from a nearby intersection after clipping a car and pushing it off the road.
