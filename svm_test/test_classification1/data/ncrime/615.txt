Modi tweets to take India-Bangladesh relationship to new level
Expressing determination to take the bonding between Bangladesh and India to a new height, Indian prime minister Narendra Modi greeted Bangladesh prime minister Sheikh Hasina to his country.

Modi, on his tweeter account welcoming Sheikh Hasina, said ‘PM Sheikh Hasina and I are determined to take the relationship between our nations to a new level.’

Indian prime minister tweeted this after receiving Sheikh Hasina at Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi

Sheikh Hasina reached New Delhi on Friday afternoon on a four-day state visit to India.
