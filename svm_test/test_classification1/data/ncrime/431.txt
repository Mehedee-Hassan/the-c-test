Annular solar eclipse on Feb 26
An annular eclipse of sun will occur on February 26, said an ISPR press release on Thursday.

It will start at 18 hours 10 minutes 54 seconds (BST) and will end at 23 hours minutes 36 minutes 00 seconds.



The central eclipse will start at 19 hours 16 minutes 48 seconds and will end at 22 hours 30 minutes 54 seconds.

The greatest eclipse will occur at 20 hours 53 minutes 24 seconds, the release said.
