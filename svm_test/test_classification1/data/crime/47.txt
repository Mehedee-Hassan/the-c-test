'Thief' beaten to death
An alleged thief was beaten to death at Podughar in Bandar upazila yesterday morning. Bandar police told UNB that Salimullah, 25, son of Ismat Ali of Podughar, was called out of his home by a group of locals and mercilessly beaten up. Badly wounded, he succumed on way to Narayanganj General Hospital. Later, police recovered his body and sent it to hospital morgue for autopsy. Salimullah was wanted in a number of cases including arms peddling and burglary.
