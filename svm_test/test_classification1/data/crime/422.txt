Three held for HSTU entry test fraud
Authorities of Hajee Danesh Science and Technology University (HSTU) yesterday caught three students over fraudulence in the university admission tests.

The frauds are Samapta Gyne, 19, son of Sukurmar Gyne and Reazul Islam, 22, son of Anwarul Islam of Narail Sadar upazila, and Ashraful Islam, 21, son of Shahidullah of Narsingdi.

Mominul Islam, deputy director of public relations department of the university, said the three got caught in the act during the first-year honours administration tests held yesterday.

HSTU authorities later handed the three over to Dinajpur Police Station, said Redwanur Rahim, officer-in-charge of the police station.
