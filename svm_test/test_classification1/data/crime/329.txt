3 indigenous people kidnapped in Bandarban
Three indigenous people were kidnapped by miscreants in Bandarban Hill Districts Monday night.

The victims were identified as Ramthan Karbari, 60, Lal Mun Karbari, 45, and Beng Tanchangya.

According to local people, a gang of miscreants entered Roniin Para village under Roangchhari upazila with heavy arms and took them away at gunpoints.

The kidnappers are reportedly member of Democratic Party Of Arakan (DPA), an outlaw group of Myanmar.

Security authorities have confirmed the incident.
