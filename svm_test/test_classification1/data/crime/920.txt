Murder case accused stabbed to death in Comilla
Miscreants stabbed a murder case accused to death at Gouripur Bazar of Daudkandi upazila in Comilla on Saturday night.

Police said miscreants attacked Abu Sayeed, 24, a resident of village Pennai in the upazila, and stabbed him indiscriminately while he along with his associate Mohammad Ali was returning home around 8:30pm, leaving him seriously injured.

The attackers also stabbed Ali as he tried to save him.

The injured were admitted to Gouripur Hospital from where Sayeed was being moved to Dhaka, but he died on the way.

A case was filed.
