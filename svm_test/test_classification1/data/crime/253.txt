9 members of ‘kidnapping gang’ held in Dhaka
Rapid Action Battalion (Rab) claimed to have detained nine alleged members of a kidnapping gang in Agargaon area of Dhaka last night.

The elite force also rescued a victim from the clutch of the kidnappers, an SMS floated to media from Rab headquarters reads.

Some firearms and bullets were recovered during the drive, it said.
