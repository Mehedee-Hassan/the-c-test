Banker suspended on forgery charge
A banker was suspended here on charge of misappropriation of bank money through forgery of cheques.

Former manager of the Sylhet City Corporation branch of Sonali Bank, Md Nuruzzaman Shahriar was suspended for misappropriating Tk 87 lakh by sanctioning loan to subscribers through cheques. But actually he had deposited Tk 7 lakh only, officials said.
