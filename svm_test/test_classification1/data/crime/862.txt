Case filed over murder of BCL leader in Satkhira
A case was filed with Satkhira police station on Tuesday night in connection with the killing of Bangladesh Chhatra League leader Hasibul Hasan Emon.

Emon's uncle Alam filed the case, said officer-in-charge of the police station Firoz Hossain Mollah.

On Tuesday morning, Emon was found dead in a fish enclosure in Amtola Beel area of Dhulihar union in Sadar upazila. He was the joint secretary of the district unit BCL.

Locals spotted Emon's body in the area around 10:00am and informed police.

Later, police recovered the body and sent it to Satkhira Hospital for autopsy.
