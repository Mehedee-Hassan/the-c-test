Four held over CU student murder
Police arrested four people and claimed on Wednesday that they ascertained the mystery of the murder of Md Alauddin, a student of Chittagong University, a week after he was murdered.

Alauddin was killed over an extra marital relationship with a married young woman, police said.

Police on Tuesday detained the woman Yasmin Akter Rumpa, 21, her husband Md Iqbal Hossain, 27, Md Tayub, 32, and Md Helal, 19 as suspects.
