'Create awareness against eve teasing'




Gaibandha district community policing committee yesterday brought out a big procession in the town to create public awareness against eve teasing.

The rally was attended by students, NGO workers and women.

Processionists paraded the main throughfares of the town.

After the procession, a discussion meeting was held at Gaibandha Public Library Hall which was presided by M Abdus Salam, convener of Gaibandha community policing committee. DIG of Rajshahi range Md Mokhlesur Rahman was present as chief guest.

The speakers called upon the all to resist the evil practice once for all from the society.

Among others, Gaibandha Police Superintendent Mollah Nazrul Islam addressed the meeting.
