US 'drunk driver' kills 4 at university rally
A car travelling at high speed driven by a suspected drunk driver has crashed into a crowd of spectators during a university parade in the US state of Oklahoma, killing four people and injuring 44 others, police said on Saturday. A local woman, 25, has been arrested on suspicion of drunk driving after her vehicle hit the crowd in Stillwater. Five children and three adults remain in hospital, some in critical condition, officials said yesterday.
