Yet again man held at Dhaka airport smuggling gold in rectum
Customs officials yesterday morning detained a person with eight gold bars weighing around 800 gram inside his body at the capital's Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport.

Tipped-off, officials held Mohammad Kamal of Comilla after he arrived at the airport from Kuala Lumpur, said customs authorities. The gold bars, around Tk 40 lakh, were inside his rectum, they said adding, they suspect that Kamal might be linked to a gold smuggling racket.
