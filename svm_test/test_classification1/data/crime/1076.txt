Burglary at Gaibandha ADC's house
Thieves took away cash, gold ornaments and other valubles worth about Tk three lakh from the hosue of additional deputy commissioner (revenue) at Masterpara in the district town early yesterday.

Police said a gang entered the house, breaking open the door and looted over five tolas of gold ornaments, Tk 54000 and a suitcase from the room of ADC Aleya Khatun.

The burglary took place at midnight when the family members were in deep sleep.

On inforamtion, Depty Commissioner Abdus Samad and the officer-in-charge of Sader Police Station visited the house.
