2 detained with bomb making materials in Cox’s Bazar
Rapid Action battalion members claimed to have detained two persons and seized bomb making materials from their possession in Cox’s Bazar today.



Acting on a tip-off, Rab-7 members conducted a raid in Kurusku Rastar Matha area of the town and detained the duo around 12:30pm, our local correspondent reports quoting Lt Commander Ashikur Rahman, commanding officer of the Rab-7.



Details will be made public at a press briefing scheduled to be held later in the day, Rab-7 CO Rahman said.
