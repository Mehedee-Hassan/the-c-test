Man jailed for smuggling gold
A Chittagong court yesterday sentenced a man to five-year imprisonment in connection with the recovery of 25 gold bars from his possession in 2015.

The convict -- Absar Mia, 31, of Raozan upazila in the district -- was also fined Tk 10,000 and in default of which he will have to serve two more months in prison. He is now in jail.

According to the prosecution, the gold bars, worth Tk 1.25 crore, were recovered from his travel bag after he arrived at Shah Amanat International Airport from Mascot on August 15, 2015.

Following the incident, Customs officials filed a case with Patenga Police Station.
