Bomb blast in front of police station
Criminals exploded a crude bomb in front of Moynamoti Highway Police Station on Dhaka-Chittagong highway last night, police said.

A few minutes later, law enforcers recovered a crude bomb in a bag from the same spot around 8:45pm. No injuries were reported.

“The explosion created panic among local people. A team of bomb disposal unit was examining the recovered bomb,” Abdullah Al Mamun, additional superintendent of Comilla police, told reporters.

Law enforcers could not confirm the motive for the bomb explosion.
