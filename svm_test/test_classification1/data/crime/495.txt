BNP activist sent to jail in cheque forgery case in Kishoreganj
A BNP activist has been sent to the jail yesterday when produced before the court in connection with a bank cheque forgery case.

Salahuddin Ahmed Selu was arrested by the DB police from Gaital area in the district town around at 12:00pm.

Police said Selu was picked up on arrest warrant in a bank cheque forgery case filed in Dhaka Sessions Judge's Court.

A retired captain of Navy, Selu has already served one year in prison with a fine of Tk 30 lakh in a similar case. Yet in another case of cheque forgery, he came out of the prison on bail recently, police added.
