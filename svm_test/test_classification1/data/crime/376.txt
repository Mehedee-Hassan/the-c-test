3 frauds held with fake passports, visas in Dhaka
Rapid Action Battalion claimed to have arrested three members of a fraud gang involved in making fake passports, visas and air tickets from Dhaka’s Sayedabad area last night.



A team of Rab-10 recovered fake passports, visas, air tickets and materials used in forging those during the drive, according to a text message floated to media by Rab today.



A media brief will be held in this regard at Rab media centre in Karwan Bazar later in the day.
