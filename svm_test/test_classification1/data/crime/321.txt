5 fishermen kidnapped in Bagerhat
Pirates looted valuables worth Tk 10 lakh from eight boats and kidnapped five fishermen for ransom from Kachikhali, Dublarchar and Narikelbaria areas in between Mongla and Sharankhola upazilas on Friday night.

Fishermen said a gang of pirates numbering 15/16 swooped on their boats one after another and looted fishing nets, fishes and other valuables while they were fishing at the Bay.

The bandits also beat the fishermen indiscriminately as they tried to resist them, leaving five of them injured.

They kidnapped five fishermen demanding Tk 2 lakh as ransom. The pirates also threatened to kill them if their demand is not met within next five days.
