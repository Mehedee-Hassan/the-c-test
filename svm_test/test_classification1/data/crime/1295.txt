3 civil staffs of army held on bribery charge
Army personnel detained three of their civil staffs on charge of taking bribe and handed over them to Kafrul police last night.

Police said auditors Zayedul Islam and Munshi Shahedur Rahman and super Abdul Khaleque were caught red handed while they were receiving Tk 4,250 from barber Norendra Kumar Chandra with assurance of clearing his bills at log area of the cantonment at 4:00pm. Hair cutting bills of Tk 7,700 have been lying unpaid for several months.

The bribe money was recovered from their possession.
