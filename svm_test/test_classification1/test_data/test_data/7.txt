Slums harbouring criminals to be demolished: IGP
The inspector general of police (IGP), AKM Shahidul Hoque, on Saturday said the authorities will evict any slums where there are criminals.



The police chief made the announcement at a programme organised at the capital’s Titumir College as a part of the campaign against militancy and drugs.



“We’ll not keep any slums where there are drug peddlers and criminals,” said Shahidul.
