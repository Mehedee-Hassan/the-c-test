Leguna driver dies after train collision
The driver of a human hauler, popularly known as Leguna, succumbed to his injuries early Sunday after a train hit his vehicle on the Mahakhali level crossing on Saturday night.

After the accident that took place around 10pm, the driver, Md Jasim, 30, was rushed to Dhaka Medical College Hospital where he died around 12:45am.
