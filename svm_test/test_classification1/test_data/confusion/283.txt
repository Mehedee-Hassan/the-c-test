Terrorist incidents interlinked: Home minister
Home minister Asaduzzaman Khan Kamal linked the incident of Bagmara mosque blast with the previous terrorist attacks in the country. An international quarter is pulling strings from behind in the incidents, he added.



The minister said this at the annual general meeting of the Crime Reporters’ Association in the Dhaka Reporters’ Unity auditorium on Saturday.



Asaduzzaman said plot has been hatched to unstable the country’s situation. The previous terrorist acts were investigated, and this (Bagmara) incident also will be investigated, he added.
