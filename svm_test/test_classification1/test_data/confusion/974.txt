Jamaat to stage demo Sunday
Bangladesh Jamaat-e-Islami on Friday announced a nationwide demonstration programme for Sunday protesting at the “government conspiracy to kill its leaders including its amir Matiur Rahman Nizami.”

“As part of its blueprint to annihilate party’s leadership, the government is conspiring to kill Matiur Rahman Nizami,” said Jamaat acting secretary general Shafiqur Rahman announcing the Sunday’s demonstration programme in a press statement.

Demanding Nizami’s release, Shafiqur Rahman said, “I, on behalf of the party, announcing peaceful demonstration programme across the country for Sunday.”
