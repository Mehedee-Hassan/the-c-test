4.2kg gold seized at Dhaka airport
Customs officials of Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport in Dhaka recovered 36 gold bars weighing 4 kilogram 200 gram on Monday morning.

The gold worth around Tk 2 crore was seized from a Fly Dubai Airline jet at about 9:00am.

Dhaka Customs House assistant commissioner Kamrul Hassan told Prothom Alo that they were informed earlier about the smuggling. They found the gold under a seat and from the toilet of the aircraft in a drive.

Customs authorities could not arrest any one in this connection. But Kamrul Hassan said that legal actions are under process regarding the matter.
