Trial begins against 60 BNP activists in Ctg
A Chittagong court on Sunday framed charges against 60 leaders and activists of Chittagong city BNP including its general secretary Shahadat Hossain.

The trial began in the court of Chittagong Metropolitan Magistrate Syed Mashfiqul Islam by pressing charges against the BNP leaders and activists in a case filed under Speedy Trial Act.

City's Kotwali police filed the case on charges of vandalism, arson, sabotage and obstructing police from discharging their duties near city's Nasiman Bhaban BNP office on December 12, 2012.
