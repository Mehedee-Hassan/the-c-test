Joypurhat housewife killed after rape
A housewife was killed and raped by miscreants at Deora village in Akkelpur upazila of Joypurhat early Sunday.

The deceased was identified as Aroti Rani, 27, wife of Ujjal Chandra, a resident of the village.

Sirajul Islam, officer-in-charge of Akkelpur police station, said Aroti did not return to the house after going out to respond to a call of nature around 10:00pm on Saturday.

After a frantic search, family members found her critically injured at a paddy field near the house around 4:00am, reports UNB news agency.

Later, she died on the way to hospital.

On information police recovered the body.

She might have been killed after rape, added the OC.
