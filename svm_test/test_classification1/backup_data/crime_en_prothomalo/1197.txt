Verdict on Sayedee appeal tomorrow
The verdict on the appeal against the death penalty awarded to war crime convict Jamaat-e-Islami leader Delawar Hossain Sayedee is expected to be delivered tomorrow.

The case has been enlisted on the top of Wednesday's cause list, according to the Supreme Court's official website.

A five-member bench of the Appellate Division headed by Chief Justice Md Muzammel Hossain is supposed to announce the verdict.
