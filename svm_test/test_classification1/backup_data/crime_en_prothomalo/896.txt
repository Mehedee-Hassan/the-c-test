Shibir man hurt while making bomb
An activist of Islami Chhatra Shibir was injured in a blast while making a crude bomb at his residence at Chandana in Gazipur.

The injured, Abdul Alim, 30, was admitted to Gazipur Shaheed Tajuddin Ahmad Medical College Hospital.

Alim is the younger brother of Abdullah Al-Basir Ahmed, Jamaat’s former defeated councilor contestant at ward 17 in the Gazipur City Corporation elections.

Joydevpur police officer-in-charge Khandakar Rezaul Hasan said they recovered bomb making materials from Alim's residence.

Police assume he was making crudes bombs to conduct acts of sabotage.
