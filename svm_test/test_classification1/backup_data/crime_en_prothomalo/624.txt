Nur Hossain's car driver Jubair arrested
Narayanganj seven-murder mastermind Nur Hossain's car driver Md Jubair was held at about 3:30am on Wednesday in Comilla.

Chandina Police arrested him from his father-in-law's house at Belashwar in Chandina municipality of Comilla district.

Chandina police officer-in-charge Golam Morshed said Jubair had been staying at his father-in-law's house for one moth. A team of Narayanganj police was coming to take him to Narayanganj, he added.
