Miscreants kill one in Khagrachhari
A man has been killed and his daughter injured as miscreants opened fire on them at Khagrachhari today.

Local people said Joy Kumar Chakma, 50, was shot dead and Kalpana Rani Chakma sustained bullet injury in the attack at Lakkhichhari upazila headquarters around 7:45am.



Assistant superintendent of police of Ramgarh circle Md Shahjahan said the Parbatya Chattagram Janasanghati Samity and United People's Democratic Front activists exchanged fire. But he could not confirm Joy Kumar's party name.
