ACC sues DG Food for alleged graft
The Anti-Corruption Commission (ACC) has filed a case against director general of Food Ahmed Hossain Khan for allegedly committing corruption in a project of the World Bank.

ACC deputy director M Mafidul Islam filed the case against him with Shahbagh Police Station in Dhaka on Sunday night.

The anti-graft watchdog's at a meeting on Sunday morning gave approval for filing the case against Ahmed Hossain Khan, ACC public relations officer Pranab Kumar Bhattacharya told UNB.

According to the case statement, Ahmed Hossain Khan along with his family members visited a foreign country taking illegal benefit from a project of the World Bank.

Finding his involvement in the corruption during an investigation of the ACC, the case was filed against Ahmed Hossain, the ACC public relations officer.
