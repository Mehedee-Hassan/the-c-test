Bank guard slaughtered in Thakurgaon
Robbers slaughtered a security guard during a reported robbery attempt at Farabarihat branch of Rajshahi Krishi Unayan Bank (RAKUB) in Sadar upazila in Thakurgaon on Tuesday evening, reports UNB news agency.

The deceased was identified as Rabiul Islam, 27.

Branch manager of the bank Taufiqul Islam said he was informed by locals over phone that some miscreants broke the door of the branch for robbery and slaughtered Rabiul.

The gangsters tried to break the vault but failed. They fled the scene before locals reached the spot, he said.

There was evidence of making an attempt to break the vault, the branch manager added.
