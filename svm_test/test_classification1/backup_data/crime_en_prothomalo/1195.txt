Bagerhat war crime suspects face 8 charges
A total of 8 charges have been brought against Sirajul Haque alias Siraj Master, Abdul Latif Talukdar and Khan Mohammad Akram Hossain of Bagerhat for their involvement in crimes against humanity during the Liberation War.

The investigators revealed this at a press briefing at the team's Dhanmondi office in Dhaka today. A total of 64 people have been enlisted as witnesses against the accused who are now in jail.

The investigation team of the International Crimes Tribunal has finalised its investigation report against the trio. The charges include murder, religious conversion, looting and arson.
