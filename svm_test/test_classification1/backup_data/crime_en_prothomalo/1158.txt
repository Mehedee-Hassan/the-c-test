Rana confesses involvement in 7-murder
RAB 11 former personnel lieutenant commander MM Rana gave confessional statement on Thursday admitting his involvement in 7-murder of Narayanganj.

District Lawyer Association president Shakhawat Hossain and Narayanganj additional prosecution lawyer Fazlur Rahman confirmed the confession to Prothom Alo.

Shakhawat Hossain told Prothom Alo we have told that lieutenant commander Rana admitted his involvement in 7-murder in a confessional statement given in the room of senior judiciary magistrate K M Mohiuddin.
