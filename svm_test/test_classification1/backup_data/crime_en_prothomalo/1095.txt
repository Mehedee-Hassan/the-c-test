One killed in Magura 'gunfight'
An alleged robber was killed in 'gunfight' with the police in Moghirdal area of Magura sadar upazila early Saturday.

The deceased was identified as Rafiqul Islam, 40, alias Moazzem.

The police said the victim was a ringleader of inter-district robbers and was accused in 29 robbery and murder cases.

He was engaged in a highway robbery at 2:15am along with his cohorts, the police also claimed.

The robbers fired bullets and hurled petrol bombs at the police as the law enforcers came to patrol the area, police claimed.

Four police members were injured during the shootout, the police added. Rafique died in police firing.
