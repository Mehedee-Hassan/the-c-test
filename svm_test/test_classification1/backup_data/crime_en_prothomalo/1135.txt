Samiul murder prime accused Kamrul sent to jail
A Sylhet court on Friday sent Kamrul Islam to jail in a case in connection with the killing of teenager Samiul Alam Rajon.

Kamrul is the prime accused in the murder case.

Sylhet chief metropolitan magistrate court-2 judge Anwarul Haque passed the order after police produced Kamrul before the court.

Assistant commissioner (Prosecution) of Sylhet metropolitan police Abdul Ahad Chowdhury told Prothom Alo that Kamrul was presented before the court around 10:45am.

Kamrul was brought back home from Saudi Arabia on Thursday.
