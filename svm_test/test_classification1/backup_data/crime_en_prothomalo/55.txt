ACC arrests 3 NU officials over graft charges
An Anti-Corruption Commission (ACC) team has arrested three officials of the National University from different parts of Dhaka over embezzlement of millions of taka.



The ACC arrested the university's proctor and deputy registrar HM Tayhid Zaman and assistant registrar Md Siddiqur Rahman and university assistant director Mofazzal Hossain on Monday morning.



According to a case statement, the accused swindled more than Tk 10.4 million disobeying a ministry ban.



ACC assistant director Fazlul Bari is investigating the case.
