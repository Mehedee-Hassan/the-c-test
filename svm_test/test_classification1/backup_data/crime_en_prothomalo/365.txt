4 held with 12kg gold in Savar, city
Members of Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) arrested four people along with 12 kilograms of gold from Tantibazar of the capital and Aminbazar in Savar, on the city’s outskirts.



The drive was conducted from Thursday noon till Friday morning.



The identities of the arrestees could not yet be known but RAB claimed that they are members of an international gold smuggling syndicate.
