Man killed in Ctg allegedly for mobile phone theft
A young man was beaten to death allegedly for stealing mobile phone set at Shitalpur village of Sitakunda upazila in Chittagong early Wednesday.



The deceased was Abdul Malek, 26, a resident of Comilla.



Police arrested two people in this connection.



Police and locals said some youngmen held Abdul Malek as a mobile phone thief and beat him severely at Chowdhury Ghat on Tuesday night and he died on the spot.



The law enforcers later recovered the body on Wednesday morning, said Sitakunda police officer in-charge Iftekar Hasan.
