‘Robber’ killed in ‘gunfight’ with rivals
A suspected robber was killed in an alleged gunfight between two groups of 'robbers' at Char Afzal in Ramgati upazila in Lakshmipur early Tuesday.

The deceased was identified as Majnu, son of Jahangir Hossain, a resident of the village.

Lakshmipur police superintendant ASM Mahatab Uddin said a gunfight between two groups of 'robbers’ - one led by Fakir and the other by Majnu -broke out at the village around 3.30am, leaving one of them dead on the spot, reports news agency UNB.

Informed, police went to the spot and recovered the body of Majnu in the morning.

The body was sent to Laxmipur Sadar Hospital morgue for autopsy.

Police also recovered one pipe gun and some bullet shells from the spot.

Majnu was wanted in 14 cases including those for robbery and kidnapping.
