Ena official arrested for signature forgery
An official of a developer firm has been arrested at Secretariat in the capital on Thursday on charge of signature forgery.

Police arrested Bulbul Ahmed, an official of Ena Properties, at the ministry of public works for his alleged counterfeiting of signature of a deputy secretary.

Ministry sources said Bulbul put a counterfeit signature of housing public works ministry deputy secretary Shaila Farzana in a letter which asked the company customers to deposit fee for handing over flats of an 8-storey apartment building in Dhanmondi area in Dhaka.

Police also arrested Belal Hossain, an employee of energy ministry at Secretariat, for selling Secretariat entry pass to Bulbul.
