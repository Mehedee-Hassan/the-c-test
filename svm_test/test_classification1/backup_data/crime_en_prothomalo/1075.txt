Man killed in Comilla 'gunfight'
A suspected robber was killed in a reported gunfight with police in Burichang upazila of Comilla early Wednesday.

The deceased is Mostafa.

District's Detective Branch (DB) police officer-in-charge KM Monjurul Alam said the policemen have also detained two robbery suspects with arms and sharp weapons from the spot.

Body of the deceased was taken to Burichang police station.
