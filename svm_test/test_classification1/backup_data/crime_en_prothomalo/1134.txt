Blogger Rajib murder case verdict any day
The High Court reserved its verdict in the case related to blogger Ahmed Rajib Haider murder after the hearing on the death reference and appeal petition of the accused ended on Monday.

A High Court bench comprised of justice Jahangir Hossain and Md Jahangir Hossain concluded the 22-day hearing.

Assistant attorney general Atiqul Haque Salim told Prothom Alo verdict will be delivered any day.

On 15 February, 2013, Ahmed Rajib Haider, an activist of Ganojagoran Mancho, was hacked to death near his house at Mirpur Palash Nagar in the capital.

On 31 December, 2015, a Dhaka court sentenced two people to death for the murder.
