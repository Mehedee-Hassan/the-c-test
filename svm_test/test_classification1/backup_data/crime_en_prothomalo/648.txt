ACC arrests 2 Feni graft suspects
The Anti-Corruption Commission (ACC) on Thursday arrested two graft suspects from Feni in separate cases.

ACC investigators of its Noakhali office detained Keramat Hossain and Mir Hossain Bhuiyan of Feni Sadar, ACC public relations officer Pranab Kumar Bhattacharya told UNB.

Keramat Hossain was arrested in a case filed against him on 26 July, 2015 for allegedly swindling over Tk 6 lakh from Sonali Bank's Feni branch.

Mir Hossain Bhuiyan was detained in a Tk 4 lakh corruption case.
