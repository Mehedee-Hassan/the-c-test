Police SP in trouble taking mango
Chapainawabganj police superintendent, Md Bashir Ahmed, is in trouble for taking some mango, and he will face Anti Corruption Commission (ACC) on this charge.

According to ACC sources, a bribery complaint, submitted on 13 September, says that Bashir received 160 baskets of mango worth Tk 90,700 from a party for providing them with unethical favour.

ACC began investigation taking the complaint in cognizance, along with some other allegations.
