Youth injured while carrying crude bomb
A youth carrying crude bomb was injured at an explosion in Khilgaon Railgate area in the city on Sunday.



The victim was identified as Mohammad Kabir, 30. The incident happened at 10:30am.



Khilgaon police officer-in-charge Mostafiz Bhuiyan said Kabir works in a private company as a marketing officer. He was carrying a carton to Islampur from Khilgaon Aman Ullah super market in the morning.



He was injured as the crude bomb kept inside the carton exploded. He has been taken to Dhaka Medical College Hospital under police protection.

However, the police could not initially say how many crude bombs were there in the carton.
