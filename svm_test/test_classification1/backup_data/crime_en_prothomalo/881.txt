Official withdrawn after bribery video goes viral
Mohammad Shahidul Haque, a government official who was caught in video while taking bribe, was withdrawn from his post on Sunday.

He was the Dhaka controller of the Chief Controller of Imports and Exports (CCIE) office under the commerce ministry.

According to an order of the ministry, Shahidul is no more holding the post, and has been attached to the ministry.

Commerce secretary Hedayetullah Al Mamun confirmed the decision to Prothom Alo.

An investigation into the bribery allegations has already been launched, he said.

Chief Controller of Imports and Exports, Mojibur Rahman, said a letter has been issued releasing Shahidul. He is no more an official of the department.
