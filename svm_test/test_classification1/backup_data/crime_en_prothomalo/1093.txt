18 suspected robbers held in city
Police have detained a gang of 18 suspected robbers in Jatrabari area of the city.

Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) said this in an SMS sent to media houses on Thursday morning.

According to the text message, the police held the robbers while they were preparing for a robbery in the area. The robber gang has been conducting robbery for a long time, the text message added.

Police claimed to have recovered 40 mobile phones, six laptops, an electronic tad and a few local arms.

The police did not unveil any further details of the detainees in the SMS.

The DMP will disclose the details of the drive in a press conference to be held today at 11:00am.
