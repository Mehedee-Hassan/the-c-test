Election officer injured in crude bomb attack
An assistant presiding officer has been injured in a crude bomb attack by miscreants in Nadna union of Sonaimuri upazila in Noakhali.



Voting has been suspended in the polling station after the attack on Debpur Government Primary School centre around 11:15am on Saturday during Union Parishad elections.



The injured, Babul Chandra Acharya, has been admitted to Sonaimuri Upazila Health Complex.
