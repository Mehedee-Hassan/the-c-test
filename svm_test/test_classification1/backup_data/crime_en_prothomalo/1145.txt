2 more held in SP’s wife murder
Chittagong Metropolitan Police have arrested two more people in connection with police superintendent Babul Akter’s wife Mahmuda Khanam Mitu murder case.



Investigating officer of the case, assistant commissioner of Chittagong detective branch (DB) Md Kamruzzaman told this to Prothom Alo on Friday noon.
