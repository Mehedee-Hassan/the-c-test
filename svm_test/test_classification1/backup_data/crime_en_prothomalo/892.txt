3 siblings held with bomb-making materials in Jessore
Three siblings were arrested with bomb-making materials and Jihadi books from the town's Shankarpur area on Saturday afternoon.

The arrestees are Rabiul Islam, son of Abdul Jabbar, hailing from Khulna district, and his sisters-Maisha Bilkis and Nusrat Parveen.

Tipped off, a team of police raided the house of one Taiebur Rahman and arrested them with the Jihadi books and bomb-making materials around 5:30pm, said Officer-in-charge (Investigation) of Kotwali police station Abul Basha.
