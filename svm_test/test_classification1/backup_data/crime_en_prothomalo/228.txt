BNP leaders Khokon, Bakul freed from jail
Bangladesh Nationalist Party (BNP) joint secretary general Barrister Mahbub Uddin Khokon, also Supreme Court Bar Association (SCBA) general secretary, and former BNP lawmaker Sakhawat Hossain Bakul were released from Kashimpur jail on Wednesday morning.

Local BNP leaders welcomed the two leaders at the jail gate as they walked out of the jail about 11:00am.

The High Court on Monday granted six-month interim bail to Mahbub Uddin in three separate cases under the Explosives Substance Act.

Mahbub Uddin, who was arrested from a place near Fisheries Building in Dhaka on 26 December last year and later shown arrested in three separate cases filed against him under the Explosives Substance Act.

Besides, Detective Branch arrested Sakhawat Hossain Bakul in front of the BNP Chairperson’s residence in Gulshan on 25 December. He was shown arrested in a vandalism case filed with the Bhatara police in Dhaka.
