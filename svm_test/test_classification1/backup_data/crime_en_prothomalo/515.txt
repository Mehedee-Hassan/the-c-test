Foreigner, 3 bankers held in ATM scam
Detectives have detained a German national for his alleged involvement in the recent fraud in ATM booths of a few banks. Three officials of City Bank were also held in this connection.



The foreigner named Thomas Peter was picked up from Dhaka city’s posh Gulshan area on Sunday night, detective branch’s deputy commissioner Sheikh Nazmul Alam confirmed to Prothom Alo on Monday morning.



The bank officials detained are Morshed Alam Maksud, Rezaul Karim and Reaz Ahmed.



Details of the arrest and the scam would be briefed soon, according to the police. Swindlers drew cash from four booths of three private commercial banks in Dhaka recently.
