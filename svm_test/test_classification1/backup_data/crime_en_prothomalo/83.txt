EC blames candidates for polls violence
Election commissioner Shah Nawaz has blamed the candidates for the violence that marred the recent upazila council elections.

“Violence occurred as the candidates relied on muscle power than on voters. It is their disqualification,” he told reporters at his office on Thursday.

He underplayed the criticisms directed the EC saying the commission took measures to arrange elections in a fair manner. “So, we’ve nothing to lose if anyone questions our transparency.”
