Sexagenarian man killed in Jamalpur
A sexagenarian man was killed by miscreants at Baburchar village in Islampur upazila early Wednesday.

The victim was identified as Somed Ali, 60, a resident of Jhigatala village of the upazila. He was a former member of Kulkandi Union Parishad.

Officer-in-charge of Islampur Police Station Din-E-Alam said Somed might have been gunned down around 12:30am.

However, it is still not clear what prompted the miscreants to kill Somed, the OC added.
