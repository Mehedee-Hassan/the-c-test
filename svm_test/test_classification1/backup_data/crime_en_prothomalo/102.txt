Wife sues Khaleda’s press secretary
BNP chairperson Khaleda Zia's press secretary Maruf Kamal Khan Sohel was sued on Monday night on charge of torturing his wife.

Sohel’s wife Tania Khan filed the case with Mohammadpur police station.

In the first information report (FIR), Tania alleged that her husband has long been maintaining ‘illicit’ affairs with another woman, said sub-inspector Tareque Zaman, also investigation officer of the case.

According to the police official, the couple had an altercation over the issue and at one stage Tania was hit by her husband on the head.

She had received medical treatment at Suhrawardy Medical College Hospital, he added.

The officer-in-charge of Mohammadpur police station, Jamal Uddin Mir, confirmed that Tania filed a case of domestic violence against Sohel at around 11:00pm.

The police are trying to arrest Sohel for the case, he added.

Maruf Kamal Khan could not be reached for his statement.
