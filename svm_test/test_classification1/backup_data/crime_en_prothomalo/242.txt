N'ganj BNP leader Kamal held
Police arrested Narayanganj BNP general secretary ATM Kamal on Wednesday morning.

Narayanganj Model Police Station officer-in-charge (OC) Manzur Kader said Kamal was arrested when he was trying to lead a procession with some workers in Hajiganj M Circus area of the town around 7:30am.

He was a fugitive in several cases filed against him, said Manzur, adding that they would appeal to the court to take him on a remand.

Cases against Kamal include for attacking police, disrupting government activities, vehicular vandalism and car arson, said Manzur.
