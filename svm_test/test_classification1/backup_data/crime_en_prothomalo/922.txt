3 injured while defusing bomb
Two members of Army bomb disposal unit and a police constable were injured while defusing a bomb at Kotwali police station in Jessore town on Sunday afternoon.

Police said the incident took place when a bomb disposal team of Army went to defuse a bomb recovered during the regular drives of police in the police station premises, leaving Sergeant Babar Ali, Sepoy Korban Ali and constable Zafar injured.

The injured were admitted to Jessore Combined Military Hospital (CMH).
