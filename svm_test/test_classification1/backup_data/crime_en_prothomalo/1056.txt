Cricketer Rubel granted 4-week bail
The High Court granted an anticipatory bail for four-week to national cricketer Rubel Hossain.

A High Court bench, comprising justice Syed AB Mahmudul Haque and justice Akram Hosain Chowdhury, passed the order on Monday following a bail petition filed by Rubel.

On Saturday, a woman filed a rape case against Rubel with Mirpur Model police station under the Women and Children Repression Prevention Act.
