US condemns blogger Niladri murder
Washington has underscored the need for working together to counter violent extremism.

The US reiterated its position in the wake of murder of blogger Niladri Chattopadhyay in Goran, Dhaka on Friday.

The United States also condemned the gruesome murder, the fourth of such killing this year.

“This heinous act once again underscores the need to work together to counter violent extremism,” The US said in a media note issued by its embassy in Dhaka on Friday.

It said US stands with Bangladeshi people who reject this vicious act and work to protect space for freedom of expression.

In the statement the US offered condolence to the family of Niladri and support for the people of Bangladesh.
