Man 'kills wife for dowry'
A housewife was beaten to death allegedly by her husband for dowry in Boda upazila of Panchagarh on Friday.

The deceased is Mukti Rani, 25, daughter of Goynath Chandra Roy, a resident of Charpara village.

Family sources said Anukul Chandra Roy, 29, son of Narendra Chandra alias Naresh and a resident of Gederkuri Bangram village, had married Mukti five years back.

There was allegation that since after their marriage, he used to torture her for dowry from her parents.

Police and locals said Anukul beat Mukti following an altercation over the same issue on Friday afternoon, leaving her dead on the spot.

Boda police officer-in-charge Mizanur Rahman confirmed the incident.

The body was recovered and sent to Panchagarh Modern Sadar Hospital for an autopsy.
