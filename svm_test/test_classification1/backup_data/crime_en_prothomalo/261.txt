22 puja pavilions vandalised
At least 22 puja pavilions have been vandalised in 15 districts across the country, complained Bangladesh Puja celebration council president, Kajal Debnath, on Monday.

He came up with the complaints at a press conference arranged by Mahanagar Sarbajanin Puja Committee at the Dhakeshwari National Temple auditorium in Dhaka. The committee’s general secretary, Narayan Moni Saha, read out a written statement in the conference.

Among the 22 vandalised pavilions, five are in Kishoreganj, two in Khulna, Dinajpur and Barisal each while the other 11 pavilions vandalised are in Rangpur, Lalmonirhat, Madaripur, Comilla, Narail, Gopalganj, Jamalpur, Sherpur, Thakurgaon, Netrakona and Sirajganj, said Kazal.

Kazal described these acts of idol vandalism as “heartbreaking”.

Mahanagar Sarbajanin Puja Committee president called for necessary action against idol vandalism.
