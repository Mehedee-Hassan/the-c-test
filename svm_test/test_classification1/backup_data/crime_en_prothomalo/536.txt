Chinese citizen arrested for ATM fraud
Police detained a Chinese citizen allegedly for ATM fraud in Dhaka’s Elephant Road area on Wednesday morning.



The detainee was identified as Huang Ho, 37.



Police said the security guard of Prime Bank ATM booth in the area informed police after suspecting Huang Ho when he spent a long time inside the ATM booth.



Ramna police deputy commissioner Jasim Uddin said the Chinese national was taken to New market police station for interrogation.

Police are also verifying the ATM cards with him, he added.
