11 held for alleged ATM card forgery
Members of Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) detained 11 people for their suspected involvement in ATM card forgery from different parts of the city on Tuesday night.

The identities of the detainees have not been as yet, reports UNB news agency.

Tipped off, a team of the elite force conducted separate drives in different areas of the city at night and detained the 11 members of an ATM card forgery gang, said sources at the RAB headquarters.

Further details will be disclosed at a press briefing later in the day.
