CC camera captures murder in Al Arafah bank
Miscreants killed security official of the Al-Arafah Islami Bank Limited in Chittagong on Thursday. The video of the murdering was captured by the bank’s CC camera.

The still pictures taken from the CCTV shows details of the slaughtering.
