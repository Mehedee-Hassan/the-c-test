Muggers hurl bomb, snatch Tk 30 lakh
Muggers snatched Tk 30 lakh from traders hurling crude bomb in broad daylight at Dilu Road of Moghbazar area in the capital at about 10:30am on Tuesday.

Ramna divisional sub-inspector Md Iqbal said businessman Abul Kashem sent his 10-12 staff to NCC Bank's Moghbazar branch for withdrawing money.

The muggers hurled crude bomb and took away the money as Kashem's employees neared Dilu Road, added Md Iqbal.
