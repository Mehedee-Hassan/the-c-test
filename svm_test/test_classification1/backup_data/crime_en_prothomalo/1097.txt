15 vehicles robbed at a time in Ctg
About 15 vehicles were robbed on the Dhaka-Chittagong highway in Sitakunda upazila on Monday.

At least four people, including drivers, were injured in attacks by the robbers.

Locals said the bandits put barricade at Barabkunda, Chhota Kumira and Panthichhila at about 2:00am and looted valuables and cash money from the passengers and drivers of the 15 vehicles, including goods-laden trucks.

Contacted, Baro Awlia highway police officer-in-charge (OC) Syed Zakir Hossain said he heard about the incident but did not get any visible evidence in connection with the robbery.

Sitakunda police inspector (investigation) Aminul Islam said there was traffic jam due to rainfall at night. Incident of robbery might have been occurred at that time, he added.
