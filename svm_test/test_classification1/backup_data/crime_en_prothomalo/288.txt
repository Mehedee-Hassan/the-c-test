CTTC now can operate across country
The Counter Terrorism and Transnational Crime (CTTC) unit will henceforth be able to carry out operations in any part of the country.

The CTTC, specialised anti-terrorism and anti-transnational crime unit of the Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP), has been given the permission in an official order issued on 25 August.

Signed by the Inspector General of Police (IGP), AKM Shahidul Hoque, the official order said the CTTC will be able to carry out its operations in any district of Bangladesh but under three conditions.
