2 held for BRAC Bank robbery
Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) arrested two men from Narayanganj early on Wednesday for their alleged involvement in the robbery of BRAC Bank's Jaipurhat branch.

Earlier, on 26 September, burglars took away around Tk 2 crore, breaking the vault of BRAC Bank at the second floor of Shah Jahan Plaza in Jaipurhat town.
