3 hurt in Mohammadpur bomb attack
Three people were injured as miscreants hurled crude bomb at a bus in the city's Mohammadpur area on Saturday morning amid BNP-led 20-party non-stop blockade.

The injured, Nurul Islam, Hira Mia, and Muzammel Haque, were admitted to Dhaka Medical College Hospital emergency unit.

The hospital's police camp in-charge Mozammel Hoque said the injured were hit by splinters in several parts of their bodies.
