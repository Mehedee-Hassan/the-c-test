US offers full support over Xulhaz murder probe
US secretary of state John Kerry has said the United States is ready to help Bangladesh government investigate into Xulhaz Mannan murder incident.



“We offer our full support to the government of Bangladesh as they investigate these murders and bring the perpetrators to justice,” said John Kerry in a press statement on Monday.



The secretary of state also said, “We remain committed to the principles that were so important to Xulhaz, and we promise to support all those who work on behalf of tolerance and human rights in Bangladesh and around the world.”

John Kerry condemned the murder of US embassy employee Xulhaz Mannan Bangladesh.

“We are profoundly saddened by the loss of one of our own in such a senseless act of violence, and we extend our deepest condolences to Xulhaz’s family and loved ones,” he added.

US Ambassador in Dhaka Marcia Bernicat in a statement also urged the government of Bangladesh to arrest the murderers of Xulhaz and his friend.

The US ambassador said she is “devastated” by the brutal murder of Xulhaz Mannan and another young Bangladeshi.
