Youth beaten dead in Chittagong
A young man was beaten to death inside a brick kiln by its workers at Padua in Lohagarah upazila on Thursday, reports news agency UNB.

Mohammad Shahjahan, officer-in-charge of Lohagarah Police Station, said workers of the brick kiln caught Mahiuddin suspecting him as a thief around 7 am.

Later they started beating him, leaving him dead on the spot.

On information, police rushed in and arrested 14 people, including the owner of the brick kiln, Liakat, in connection with the incident.
