Wife strangulated to death by husband
A housewife was strangulated to death allegedly by her husband for dowry at Sabek Khator village in Shalikha upazila on Saturday.

The deceased was identified as Popy Khatun, 19, wife of Akash Ali of the same village.

Shalikha police officer-in-charge Biplob Kumar Nath said Akash used to torture Popy for dowry since their marriage. Popy also filed a case with a court in this connection.

Family members claimed Akash called his wife out of her father’s house at Sabek Khator village in the early hours on Saturday.

Later on Saturday noon, locals found the body of Popy in a field of the village and informed police.

On information, police recovered the body and sent it to Sadar Hospital morgue for autopsy.

Akash fled the scene after the incident.
