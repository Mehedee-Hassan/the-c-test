Alal put on 3-day remand
A Dhaka court on Sunday placed Juba Dal president, Syed Moazzem Hossain Alal, on a 3-day remand in a case filed in connection with Wednesday’s violence at Chawkbazar in the city.

Dhaka additional chief metropolitan magistrate, Ali Masud Sheikh, passed the order after rejecting his bail prayer when police produced him before the court seeking a 10-day remand.

Police arrested the president of the BNP’s youth wing from his Lalmatia residence on Saturday night.

He was shown arrested in a case filed with Chawkbazar police station for vandalism and obstructing police from their duties on December 26 at Bakshi Bazar.
