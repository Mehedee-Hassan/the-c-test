Garo girl rape accused caught again
Police arrested the main accused in the Garo girl rape case, after he escaped from police custody on Sunday.

The accused, Afsan Rahman Rubel, was arrested last Friday by the Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) who handed over him to Badda police station on Saturday.

On Sunday, the police led by sub-inspector Imranul Hassan, took Rubel to the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate's court, from where he escaped.

After the incident, the Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP), public relations and media wing said that Rubel was taken to the court for recording statement under section 164 of Criminal Procedure after he confessed to raping the ethnic woman.

SI Imranul Hassan and constable Dipok were suspended for negligence of duties.
