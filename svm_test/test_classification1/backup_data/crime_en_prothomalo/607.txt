Auto-rickshaw driver found dead in N'ganj
The beheaded body of an auto-rickshaw driver was recovered from Siddhirganj of Narayanganj on Friday.

The deceased was Abdul Karim, 28, son of Jamser Ali of Kumarbari in Palashbari upazila of Gaibandha, he had been living at Dewbhog Madrasah road in the city.

Locals spotted the body of Karim at Jalkuri Karoitala by Dhaka-Narayanganj Link Road in the morning and informed police.

Later, the law enforcers recovered the body and sent it to Narayanganj General Hospital for autopsy, said Siddhirganj police sub-inspector Masud Alam Khan.

There were marks of injury on the face of the victim, the police officer said, adding that miscreants might have taken away his three-wheeler after killing him.
