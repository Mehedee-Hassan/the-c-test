Truck driver crushed under truck
A truck driver was run over by a truck at Mirpur upazila of Kushtia early Sunday.

The deceased is Lavlu, 25, a resident of Shimulia village in Jhikargachha upazila of Jessore.

One of his relatives said a truck ran over Lavlu while he was changing wheel of his truck on the roadside at Kadamtala of Mirpur upazila at 1:30am on Friday.

Critically injured Lavlu was taken to Kushtia General Hospital where doctors declared him dead.
