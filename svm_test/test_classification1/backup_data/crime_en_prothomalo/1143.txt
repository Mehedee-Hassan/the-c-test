Highest priority for Shafiul murder case: IGP
Inspector general of police (IGP) Hasan Mahmud on Saturday said police are giving highest priority to the Rajshahi University professor Shafiul Islam murder investigation.

IGP said this while talking to reporters after the closing ceremony of the training of 34th batch of External Cadet Inspectors at Bangladesh Police Academy in Sarda.

The IGP said police will arrest the admins of the Ansar-Al-Islam Bangladesh-2, the facebook page that claimed responsibility for the murder.

He also said all the necessary measures have been taken to maintain the law and order at the Rajshahi University.
