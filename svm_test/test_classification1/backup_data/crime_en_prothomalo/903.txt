Police sergeant hurt in city bomb attack
A police sergeant got injured as miscreants hurled crude bomb at him in city's Shyamoli area on Saturday.

The splinter hit Golam Moula, a traffic police sergeant, was admitted to Shaheed Suhrawardy Medical College and Hospital.

Sher-e-Bangla Nagor police station officer-in-charge G Bishwas confirmed the incident to Prothom Alo.

"A group of 8-10 people walked to him and hurled a crude bomb at Golam Maola around 9:30am when he was on-duty in front of Shishu Mela (children park) in Shyamoli.," said the OC.

The miscreants managed to flee the scene, the OC added.
