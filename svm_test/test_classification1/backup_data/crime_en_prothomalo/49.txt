Corruption money used in militancy: ACC chief
Anti Corruption Commission chairman Iqbal Mahmud said the money obtained through corruption is used in militant activities.



He said financial anarchy and militancy is a severe problem in today’s world, and added, “We cannot be safe if we cannot tackle it simultaneously.”



He said this during a discussion with the editors and chiefs of print and electronic media about strategy and working plan of ACC held at its office in Dhaka on Sunday.



He said ACC is now mostly concerned with checking corruption in banking sector. “Thousands of crores of taka is being plundered from the banks. We want stop the embezzlement. The boards of directors of the banks are also responsible for this.”
