Gulshan massacre 'mastermind' Tamim Chy's associate arrested
A close associate of alleged Gulshan massacre mastermind Tamim Chowdhury was arrested on Wednesday.

Dhaka Metropolitan Police counter terrorism unit deputy commissioner Mohammad Muhibul Islam told Prothom Alo on Thursday that the counter terrorism and transnational crime department arrested Salauddin Kamran, 30, a close associate of Tamim Chowdhury.

Salauddin was arrested from Tongi's Station Road area in the capital, he added.

He said Salauddin was a member of banned militant outfit neo-JMB(Jamaat-ul-Mujahideen)
