7 arms dealers nabbed in capital: DB
Detective Branch (DB) of Dhaka metropolitan police held seven arms dealers carrying weapons, bullets and explosive materials at Jatrabari in Dhaka in early hours of Tuesday.

Dhaka Metropolitan police in a text message said that the police seized 5 firearms, 28 bullets, 5 magazines and 6kg of explosive materials from their possession.

Anti-Kidnapping Squad chief Sanwar Hossain conducted a raid at Jatrabari on information given by the fake DB policemen arrested on Sunday night.

Sanwar Hossain said the held group gave arms on rent. They were also involved in arms supply from the border.

There is also a female member among the seven arms dealers.
