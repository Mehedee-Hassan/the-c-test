2 more held in SP’s wife murder case
Police on Tuesday arrested two more for their alleged involvement in the murder of Mahmuda Khanam Mitu, wife of police superintendent Babul Akter.

Additional commissioner Debdas Bhattacharya of Chittagong Metropolitan Police confirmed this to Prothom Alo.

On 5 June, unidentified miscreants gunned down Mahmuda Khanam Mitu at the city’s GEC intersection while she went there to drop her son, Mahir Akhter, 7, for a bus to Chittagong Cantonment Public School and College.
