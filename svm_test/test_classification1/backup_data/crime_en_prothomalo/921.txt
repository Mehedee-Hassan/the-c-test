Crude bomb exploded in Rajshahi court
Miscreants exploded a crude bomb in Rajshahi court premises on Wednesday.

No casualties have been reported in the incident.

Witness said the bomb was exploded near Rajshahi district judge Bhaban (Zila Judge Bhaban) at 10:00 am.

Rajpara police officer-in-charge Mehedi Hasan told correspondent that police were present at the premises but the bomb went off in a vacant place.
