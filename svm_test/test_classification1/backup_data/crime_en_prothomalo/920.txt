Bomb blasts in front of police station in Comilla
A crude bomb exploded in front of Maynamati Highway Police Station on Dhaka-Chittagong highway on Monday night, reports news agency UNB.

Abdullah Al Mamun, additional superintendent of Comilla police, said the crude bomb went off in front of the police station around 8:30pm.

Besides, police recovered a live crude bomb kept in a bag from the spot around 8:45pm.

No one was injured in the blast.
