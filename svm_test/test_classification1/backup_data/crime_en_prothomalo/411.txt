9 alleged kidnappers held
The Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) have detained nine suspected members of a kidnapping gang from Dhaka on Monday.



In a short message service (SMS) sent to newsman on Monday morning, RAB said a team of RAB-3 detained nine people from Rampura.



All of them are members of a kidnapping gang. Mobile phones and SIM cards have been seized from their possession.



However, they did not provide further details.
