Man held for rape attempt on minor in capital
Police arrested a young man on Sunday on charge of rape attempt on a 7-year-old girl at Rayerbazar in Dhaka.

The girl has been taken to Dhaka Medical College Hospital for check up.

Police said a young man attempted to rape the girl in her house Saturday evening in the absence of her family members. He fled the scene as the girl screamed in fear. The family lodged a complaint with Hazaribagh police.
