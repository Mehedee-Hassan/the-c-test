Car jacked after driver stabbed
A gang of carjackers made away with a private car after stabbing its driver in downtown Kalabagan area on Thursday morning.

The driver, Masud Rana, 32, who is undergoing treatment at Dhaka Medical College Hospital, said the group of 5-6 carjackers aboard a pick-up van stopped his car and hit him with sharp weapons before snatching the key.

They also took away Masud's mobile phone and some cash before dumping the driver, who received injuries in several parts of his body.
