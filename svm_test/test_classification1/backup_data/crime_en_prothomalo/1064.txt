2 held over Dhaka gang rape
Rapid Action Battalion on Tuesday night arrested another suspect in connection with the gang-rape of a Garo woman inside a running microbus in the capital’s Kuril area a week earlier .

Major Rumman Mahmud of RAB media wing confirmed the news to Prothom Alo on Wednesday morning.

On Tuesday night, RAB claimed to have arrested Tushar, the prime suspect of the infamous rape case.

According to RAB, Lavlu, the latest arrestee, was caught from the Gulshan-1 area of the capital.
