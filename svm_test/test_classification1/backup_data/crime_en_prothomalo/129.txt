Bodies of 2 women, a child found in Jamuna
Police recovered bodies of two women and a child from the river Jamuna at Enayetpur of Sirajganj on Monday.

Enayetpur police said local people informed the police of a floating sack in the river near Khawja Eunus Ali Medical College.

Police found the bodies of two women, aged about 30-35, and a child stuffed in the sack retrieved from the river around 11:15 am.

The bodies were taken to Sirajganj General Hospital for autopsy.
