Sundari wood worth 1.25 crore seized
Bangladesh Coast Guard seized on Wednesday a large quantity of Sundari wood worth about Tk 1.25 crore in drives at Utrail hat in Madaripur district.

According to the coast guard officials, an operation team of Coast Guard West Zone started the drive at Utrail Hat, adjacent to Arial Kha river and recovered the wood kept in the Utrail hat allegedly for smuggling.

The wood was handed over to Changmari Forest office in Madaripur.
