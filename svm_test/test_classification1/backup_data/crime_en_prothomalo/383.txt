Biman staff involved in 14kg gold smuggling
The customs intelligence has got evidence of involvement of Biman employees in the 14kg gold smuggling on Wednesday.

The customs officials claimed Biman engineering division's technician Emranul Islam and Osman Gani were involved in the incident.

Assistant revenue officer Abul Hossain filed a case against the Biman workers with airport police station today, Thursday.

Earlier on Wednesday, customs officials recovered 14kg gold from a cargo hold of a Biman flight.
