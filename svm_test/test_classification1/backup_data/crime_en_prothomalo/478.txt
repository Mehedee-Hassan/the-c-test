ICT probe report suggests banning Jamaat
International Crimes Tribunal (ICT) investigation body finalised its probe report against Jamaat-e-Islami and its associate bodies for their role during the 1971Liberation War. The report has also asked the authorities concerned to ban Jamaat and its associate bodies as political parties.

The 376-page investigation report will be submitted to the prosecution office on Tuesday.

Investigation body's chief coordinator Abdul Hannan Khan said in a press conference that they have finalised a probe report against Jamaat-e-Islami and its erstwhile student wing Islami Chhatra Shangha, Shanti committee, Razakar, Al Badr, Al Shams and their mouthpiece newspaper The Daily Shangram.

Probe body brought charges over Jamaat's involvement in seven types of war crimes. The charges include crimes against humanity, genocide, war crime and so on.
