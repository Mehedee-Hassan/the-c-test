Gold recovered from arrestee in court
Police seized three gold bars from an arrestee held in a gold smuggling case at Dhaka Metropolitan court premises in Dhaka on Monday.





Police said Arif Hossain Munshi, 36, was arrested at the Hazrat Shahjalal International airport as three gold bars were recovered from him on his arrival from Malaysia on Sunday.



He was later brought to the court on Monday where police, over suspicion, recovered three more gold bars from Arif.



During interrogation, Arif admitted that the gold bars were hidden in his rectum and he took it out in the toilet of court jail.

Court jail officer in-charge Murad Hossain confirmed the incident.
