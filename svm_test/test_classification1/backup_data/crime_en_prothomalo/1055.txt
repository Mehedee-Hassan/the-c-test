Protests against sexual assaults in school
Guardians and students of the Mohammadpur Preparatory School and College on Saturday formed a human chain in front of the school demanding punishment for the staff involved in rape attempts on students on the campus.

Recently, two students of grade five and grade four have allegedly become victims of rape attempt by school staff. Guardians broke into protest on Wednesday also in front of the school against the rape attempts.
