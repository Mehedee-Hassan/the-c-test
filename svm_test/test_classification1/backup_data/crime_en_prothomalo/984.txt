Gazipur road crashes kill 2
Two people were killed in separate road accidents in Bhogra and Gazipura areas of the city on Tuesday, reports news agency UNB.

Police said Rafiqul Islam, son of Osman Ali of Natore district, was crushed under the wheels of a moving bus at Bhogra in the morning.

Another unidentified man was killed as a truck knocked him down at Gazipura on Dhaka-Mymensingh Highway.

Police later recovered the bodies and sent those to Shaheed Tazuddin Ahmad Medical College Hospital for autopsies.
