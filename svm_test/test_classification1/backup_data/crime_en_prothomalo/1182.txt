Family members meet Kamaruzzaman
The family members of convicted war criminal Kamaruzzaman met him at the Dhaka Central Jail on Monday evening.

Kamaruzzaman's wife Nurun Nahar along with her sons and daughters met the Jamaat-e-Islami assistant secretary general inside the jail around 6:45pm.

Senior jail superintendant Farman Ali said the copy of Supreme Court verdict has not reached the prison yet.

Earlier on the day, the appellate division of Supreme Court upheld the death sentence for Kamaruzzaman for his crime against humanity during the liberation war.
