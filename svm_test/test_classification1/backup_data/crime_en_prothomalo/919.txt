Crude bomb blasts in DU arts building
A crude bomb has blasted in Dhaka University's arts faculty building on Tuesday.

The explosion created panic among the students of the university but there were no casualties in the incident.

The bomb exploded on the lawn between the history department and the mass communication and journalism department.

An unexploded bomb was also recovered from the spot.

The university's acting proctor M Amzad Ali said, "We came to know the crude bomb was thrown from any upper floor of the building."

An investigation is going on to identify the perpetrator, he added.
