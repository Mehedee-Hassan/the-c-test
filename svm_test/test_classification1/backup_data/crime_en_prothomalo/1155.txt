7-murder accused Hasu arrested


Border Guard Bangladesh (BGB) members arrested Hasmat Ali alias Hasu, one of the prime accused in the 7-murder case of Narayanganj, from Jessore border area on Tuesday night.

Narayanganj district additional police superintendent and 7-murder case interrogation committee convener Md Jakaria confirmed the arrest to Prothom Alo.

Hasmat Ali Hasu is the number-3 accused in this murder case, who is a resident of Mijimiji Poshchimpara in Shiddhirganj.

Hasu is one of the accomplices of the number-1 accused in this case, councillor Nur Hossain.
