5 burned in Feni petrol bomb attack
Five people sustained burn injuries as miscreants hurled a patrol bomb on a truck in Matbhuiyan area of Daganbhuiyan Upazila in Feni on Friday early morning.

Police said the miscreants threw a petrol bomb on the Noakhali-bound truck from Comilla around 6:15 am injuring five, including the driver of the truck.

The injured were taken to Fenny Sadar Hospital immediately.

Daganbhuiyan police officer in-charge Abul Faisal confirmed the incident.

No one was arrested in the incident, he added.
