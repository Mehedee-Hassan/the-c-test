Tiger sculpture collapses, kills man in city
A van driver was killed as the sculpture of a Royal Bengal Tiger collapsed on him at Karwan Bazar intersection in Dhaka early Friday.

Identity of the van driver was not known immediately.

Fire service man Shahid said the van driver was sleeping when the sculpture suddenly fell on him leaving him critically injured.

On information, fire service men rushed him to Dhaka Medical College and Hospital where doctors declared him dead.

The body was kept at DMCH morgue for autopsy.
