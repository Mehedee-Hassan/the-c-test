2 war crime suspects held in Netrakona
Two people were held at Purbadhala upazila of Netrakona district on Wednesday morning on charges of crimes against humanity during Liberation War in 1971.

The detained were Abdur Rahman, 70, a resident of Barha Gonokpara village under Jaria union of the upazila, and Ahmad Ali, 78, a resident of

Soanikanda Ghagra union of the same upazila.

Police superintendent Joydeb Kumar Bhadra said the accused could not be shown arrested since there were no arrest warrant yet. Their identity and addresses are being verified, he added.
