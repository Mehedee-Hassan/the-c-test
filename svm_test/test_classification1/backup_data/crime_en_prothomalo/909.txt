Bomb explosion injures 4 female workers in Dhaka
A bomb explosion left four female construction workers injured at Khilgaon in Dhaka on Tuesday.

The four female construction workers suffered splinter injuries when the buried crude bomb exploded in the morning.

The bomb blasted about 10:00am while the injured four were working on road construction at road no-17, Tilpapara, said Khilgaon police.

The injured, Ambia Khatun, 33, Ratna Rani, 30, Hironbashi, 45 and Selina Begum, 50, are now undergoing treatment at Dhaka Medical College and Hospital.

The injured four were reported to be out of danger.
