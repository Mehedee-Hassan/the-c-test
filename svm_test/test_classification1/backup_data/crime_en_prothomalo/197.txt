Death for Mobarak Hossain
International Crimes Tribunal-1 on Monday sentenced former Awami League leader Mobarak Hossain todeathfor his crimes against humanity during the Liberation War in 1971.

The three-member International Crimes Tribunal 1, headed by Enayetur Rahim, announced the verdict at around 12:00 noon.

The ICT-1 gave hanging order for Mobarak on charge no 1 and life term on charge no 3. He was acquitted of the three other charges.

Earlier on April 23, the ICT-1 framed five charges, including killing, abduction, looting, and arson, against Mobarak.
