Arrest warrant against Jabbar
International Crimes Tribunal-1 on Monday issued arrest warrant against former Jatiya Party lawmaker of Matbaria constituency in Pirojpur Md Abdul Jabbar for his alleged crimes against humanity during the Liberation War of Bangladesh in 1971.



State prosecutor Zahid Imam on Sunday submitted allegations against Jabbar to the war crimes tribunal's registrar office.



The prosecution brought five charges of mass killing, looting and forced conversion in 1971 against him. The investigation against him was completed last month.



International Crimes Tribunal chairman Justice Enayetur Rahim passed the order and fixed 2 June as the next date of hearing accepting five war crimes charges against Jabbar.
