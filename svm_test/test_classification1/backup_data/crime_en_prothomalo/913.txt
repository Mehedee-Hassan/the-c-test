Bomb blasts in front of Khulna SP office
A bomb exploded in front of the Khulna superintendent of police's (SP) office on Wednesday night.

However, nobody was injured in the explosion.

Khulna sadar police station in-charge Sukumar Biswas said unknown miscreants threw the bomb that exploded near the car parking area of the SP office ground.

Previously, miscreants blasted bombs in front of the residence of Khulna range's deputy inspector general and Khulna metropolitan police head office on 29 January and 31 January respectively.
