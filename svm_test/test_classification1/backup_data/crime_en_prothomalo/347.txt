1 held with heroin in Dhaka
Detectives arrested one person along with 1.285 kg heroine from the city’s Nikunja area on Friday evening. The arrestee was identified as Saiful, a nephew and partner of Elias who was hanged in China in a case for smuggling 20kg heroine in 2014.

Deputy Commissioner (Media) of DMP Masudur Rahman said, a team of DB police conducted a drive in the area and arrested Saiful in the evening.

DB police also recovered 1.285 kg heroine from his possession which he imported from Pakistan, the DC added.
