Brothers kill brother in Comilla
A man was beaten to death allegedly by his two brothers following a family feud at Kalkhorpar village in sadar upazila of Comilla on Thursday.

The deceased is Shah Alam, son of Sanu Mia.

Chattarkhil police sub-inspector Shafiqul Islam said Shah Alam locked into an altercation with his two younger brothers —Faruk Hossain and Sharif Hossain —over family affairs.

At one stage, Faruk and Sharif beat up Shah Alam severely, leaving him dead on the spot.

Later, they dumped the body in a pond.

On information, police recovered the body and sent it to Comilla Medical College Hospital morgue for autopsy.
