Faisal’s wife files case over his murder
Slain publisher Faisal Arefin Dipan’s wife filed a case over his murder on Monday against some unnamed people.

Dipan’s wife Dr Razia Rahman filed the case with Shahbagh police station in the afternoon, said its officer-in-charge AB Siddique.

Dipan, 45, proprietor of ‘Jagriti’ publication house, was stabbed to death by miscreants on the 2nd floor of Aziz Super Market in the city’s Shahbagh area on Saturday.
