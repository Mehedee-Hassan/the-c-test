'Thief' lynched in Savar
A suspected thief was beaten to death by angry mob in Dhamrai of Savar upazila early Wednesday.

The identity of the deceased was not known immediately.

Police said locals caught a man suspected to be a thief and beat him mercilessly in Chardautia at around 3am, leaving him dead on the spot.

The police later recovered the body.
