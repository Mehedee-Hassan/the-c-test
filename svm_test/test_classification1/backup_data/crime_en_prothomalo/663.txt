ACC quizzes WASA MD
The Anti-Corruption Commission on Tuesday interrogated Dhaka WASA managing director for alleged anomalies and corruption in the organisation.

A three-member investigative team, led by ACC director Zayed Hossain Khan, quizzed WASA chief Taqsim A Khan at WASA Bhaban at Karwan Bazar in Dhaka.
