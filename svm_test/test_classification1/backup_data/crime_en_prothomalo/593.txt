Tangail road crash kills 4
Four people were killed and two others injured in a road accident in Charbhabla area of the district's Kalihati upazila early Wednesday.

Police said the accident took place when a CNG-driven autorickshaw was crushed in between two trucks at about 12:30am, leaving the autorickshaw driver and three passengers injured.

The deceased are Faruque Hossain, 35, the driver of the autorickshaw, and passengers Golam Kibria, 40, Nurul Islam, 40, and Niranjan Saha, 35.

The injured were rushed to Tangail Medical College Hospital.
