2 die in Ctg road crash
A covered van driver and his assistant were killed on Tuesday as their van hit a roadside tree beside Dhaka-Chittagong highway in Mirsarai of Chittagong.

The deceased are van driver Omar Farukh, 30, and his assistant Md Sohag, 25.

Jorarganj highway police sub-inspector Farid Uddin told Prothom Alo the Chittagong-bound garments products-laden covered van hit a roadside tree while trying to overtake an oil tanker.
