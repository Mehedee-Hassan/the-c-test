ACC arrests 2 Janata Bank DGMs
The Anti-Corruption Commission (ACC) on Thursday arrested two deputy general managers of state-run Janata Bank in cases over Bismillah Group’s Tk 1,100 crore embezzlement from the bank.

The bank’s corporate branch DGMs-Azmul Haque and SM Abu Hena Mostofa Kamal-were arrested from Motijheel. Both of them are accused in two cases each.

ACC officials confirmed the arrest to the Prothom Alo. An ACC team under the leadership of its director Mir Joynul Abedin Shibli arrested the top officials of the state-owned bank.

The ACC in 2013 filed 12 cases accusing 54 including Bismillah Group’s managing director Khaja Soleman Anowar Chowdhury, its chairman Nowrin Hasib and Prime Bank DMD Md Yeasin Ali, in connection with Tk 1,200 crore loan scam.
