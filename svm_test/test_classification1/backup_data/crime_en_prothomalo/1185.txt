Arrest warrant for 5 Gaibandha war crime suspects
The International Crimes Tribunal (ICT) on Sunday issued warrants for the arrest of five war crime suspects of Sadar upazila in Gaibandha district for their alleged involvement in crimes against humanity during the Liberation War in 1971.



A three-member tribunal, led by Justice M Anwarul Haque, passed the order following a petition filed by prosecution Hrishikesh Saha, reports UNB.

The names of the suspects were not disclosed.



Besides, the ICT showed Ranju Mia, another war crimes suspect of Gaibandha district now in jail, arrested in a war crimes case, and issued a warrant for his arrest for his involvement in crimes against humanity.

Police arrested Ranju on April 19 in another case.



The tribunal also asked the police station concerned to arrest the suspects, said the prosecutor.

The tribunal fixed July 27 for the next hearing.
