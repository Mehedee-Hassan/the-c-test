LGED engineer arrested in graft case
Anti Corruption Commission (ACC) arrested a deputy director of Local Government Engineering Department (LGED) in a graft case on Wednesday.

The arrested is Tamjid Sarwar, deputy director of urban management support unit of LGED, Dhaka.

A graft case was filed against Tamjid with Dharmapasha police station on Tuesday.

He was charged with Tk 1,02,42,334 embezzlement from Dharmapasha-Jayshree road construction project fund in 2010-2012.

He was then serving as an executive engineer in Sunamganj.
