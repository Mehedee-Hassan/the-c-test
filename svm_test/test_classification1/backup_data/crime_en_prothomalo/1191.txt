Hearing on framing charges against Sakhawat, 8 others 17 Nov
The International Crime Tribunal-1has fixed 17 November for the hearing on framing charges against war crime suspect former Jamaat-e-Islami lawmaker Mawlana Sakhawat Hossain and eight others.



The nine accused have been charged with five counts of crimes including arson, genocide and murder during the Liberation War.



A three-member ICT bench, led by Justice Anawarul Haque, passed the order on Tuesday.



Sakhawat Hossain, Billal Hossain and Lutfar Morol have been arrested in this case.



Other six accused- Ibrahim, Mujibor, Aziz Sarder, Quazi Obaidul Islam, Abdul Khaleq and Aziz Sardar-2 are absconding.
