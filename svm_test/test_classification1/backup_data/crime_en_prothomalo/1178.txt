RU teacher hacked to death
Rajshahi University English department professor Rezaul Karim Siddique has been hacked to death in Shalbagan area of the city on Saturday.



Rezaul Karim Siddique, 58, was hacked by unidentified miscreants near his house while going to catch university bus in the morning.



His brother Sazidul Karim Siddique told the correspondent that Rezaul was found dead on the road at around 7:30am.



Boalia police station officer-in-charge visited the crime scene.
