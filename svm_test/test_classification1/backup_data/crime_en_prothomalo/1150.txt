Osmani Medical College BCL president arrested
Sylhet Osmani Medical College Bangladesh Chhatra League (BCL) president Soumen Dey, also a chargeshetted accused of Jatiyatabadi Chhatra Dal (JCD) leader Tauhidul Islam's murder case, was arrested at about 10:00pm on Saturday.

Police held him from an area adjacent to Sylhet rail station.

Soumen is one of the 10 BCL activists who have been expelled from the college over the murder.

Sylhet city police additional commissioner Md. Rahamatullah confirmed the arrest.
