2 accused in Ekram murder case freed on bail
Two accused in the Phulgazi upazila chairman murder case have been released on bail today, Monday.

This is for the first time charge-sheeted accused have been released in upazila chairman Ekramul Haque murder case.

The accused, Abdur Rahman and Md Masud were released from Feni jail. The High Court granted bail to them on 2 December.

Abdur Rahman is president of Kazirbag union Awami League unit and Masud is a Juba League activist.
