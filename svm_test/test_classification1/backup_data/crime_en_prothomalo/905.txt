2 injured in bomb blast near New Market
Two passersby were injured in a bomb blast under the footbridge in front of New Market in Dhaka at 7:45pm on Saturday.

The injured Sadia Afrin, 30, and Abu Hanif, 45, were taken to Dhaka Medical College Hospital for treatment.

DMCH police camp inspector Mozammel Haque said the victims were hit by splinters of crude bombs blasted by miscreants while they were crossing the road.
