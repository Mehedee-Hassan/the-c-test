SUST student suspended on admission fraud charge
The authorities of the Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST) in Sylhet suspended a student of its Food Engineering and Tea Technology department on Sunday on charge of forgery in honours admission test.

SUST acting proctor Munshi Naser Ibne Afzal said the university authorities took the decision against Al Amin following a probe committee report, reports UNB news agency.
