Bangladesh, India to combat terrorism together
Bangladesh foreign affairs secretary Muhammad Shahidul Haque on Thursday said Bangladesh and India will henceforth combat terrorism unitedly.

The Bangladesh foreign secretary told this to journalists at the state guest house in Dhaka after a meeting with his Indian counterpart Subrahmanyam Jaishankar.



Shahidul Haque also said the bilateral relations between the two countries will strengthen further in the future.

He said his counterpart has issued an invitation of Indian prime minister Narendra Modi to Bangladesh prime minister Sheikh Hasina to join upcoming BRICS conference in India in October this year.
