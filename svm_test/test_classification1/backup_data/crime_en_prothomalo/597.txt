2 killed as truck falls into river in Chandpur
Two persons were killed when their vehicle fell into the river in Truckghat area of the Chandpur town.



The deceased were truck driver Mohammad Mostafa, 40, of Chandpur district and his assistant Mohammad Alauddin, 25, of Bhola district.



Police said the accident occurred when a rice-laden truck fell into the Dakatia river in the area after driver of the vehicle lost control over the steering.



The driver died as he was trapped inside the vehicle.



The truck helper Mostafa was rushed to the Chandpur sadar hospital where attending doctor declared him dead.



Mohammad Mamunur Rasheed, officer-in-charge of Chandpur Sadar Model thana, confirmed the incident.
