13 robbers detained, claims DB
The Detective Branch (DB) of Dhaka Metropolitan police has detained 13 robbers from city's Motijheel and Mohammadpur area.

In an SMS, the police claimed the robbers were detained while preparing for a robbery.

The text message said details of this drive will be disclosed at a press briefing at the DMP media center at 11:00am on Tuesday.

Three pistols, 13 crude bombs, one kilogram of gun powder, two microbuses and a car have been recovered in drive, the text message added.
