Driver injured as bus bombed at Shahbagh
A driver was injured as some unidentified people hurled crude bombs on his bus in Shahbagh on Monday night.

The injured, Rashedul Islam, was admitted to Dhaka Medical College Hospital.

Witnesses said miscreants attacked a United Paribahan bus with crude bombs at about 7:45pm and the driver of the bus was hit by splinters of the bombs.

Rashedul Islam, at the hospital, said he was driving to Mirpur from Sadarghat. Miscreants hurled bombs at his bus when it reached Shahbagh.
