Govt has taken up Japanese murder incident seriously
Home minister Asaduzzaman Khan Kamal said the government has taken up seriously the matter of Japanese national’s death after the Italian citizen murder in the country.

“We have taken up the matter with utmost seriousness,” he told Prothom Alo, adding that whoever involved in the murder will be brought to justice. Correlation, if any, of the two murders will be investigated, the minister added.
