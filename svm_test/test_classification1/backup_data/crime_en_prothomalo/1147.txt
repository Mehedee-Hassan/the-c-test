Ansar al-Islam condemns murder of SP’s wife
Ansar al-Islam, the Bangladesh division of al-Qaeda in the Indian Subcontinent (AQIS), condemned the killing of Mahmuda Aktar, wife of police Superintendent Babul Aktar, in Chittagong recently.





In a statement, Ansar al-Islam denounced the murder as ‘impermissible under Islam’, reported the SITE Intelligence Group on its website on Friday.
