Pak high commissioner summoned
Foreign ministry has summoned Pakistan high commissioner to Bangladesh Shuja Alam on Monday over a comment of Pakistan foreign ministry on the Supreme Court verdict that dismissed review plea of war crime convict Motiur Rahman Nizami.



Foreign secretary (bilateral) Mizanur Rahman will hand over a protest note on behalf of the government.



Shuja Alam meanwhile arrived in the ministry.
