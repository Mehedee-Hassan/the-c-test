Charges against 2 Mymensingh war crimes suspects accepted
The International Crimes Tribunal (ICT) on Tuesday accepted charges against two war crimes suspects of Mymensingh for their alleged involvement in genocide and crimes against humanity during 1971 war.

The accused are Riaz Uddin Fakir and Wajuddin Fakir of Phulbaria upazila of Mymensingh district.

A three member-bench of ICT-1 led by Justice Anwarul Haque took cognizance of five charges against them and fixed 16 May for passing next order in the case.

On 23 March, the prosecution pressed the charges against the two war crimes suspects.

On 4 September 2015, another accused of the case Haji Amjad Ali, who sustained injuries in a road accident in Gazipur city on 12 August, died at Dhaka Medical College Hospital (DMCH).
