JU teacher removed for sexual assault
AKM Anisuzzaman, faculty of Accounting and Information Systems department of Jahangirnagar University, has been removed from his post on charges of sexual assault.

Registrar Abu Bakar Siddique said the decision was taken at the syndicate meeting on Thursday.

The registrar said a number of students filed complaints against Anisuzzaman. Later, the sexual harassment cell of the university investigated the complaints and found those to be authentic.

Anisuzzaman was removed from his post based on a probe report submitted by a statutory committee comprised of the vice chancellor and the registrar of the university.

Earlier On 23 August last year, he was suspended.
