Traffic accident kills 3 in Sylhet
Three people, including an auto-rickshaw driver, were killed in a traffic accident on the Sylhet-Dhaka Highway in South Surma upazila on Tuesday.

Of the deceased, one was Salam Ahmed, an auto-rickshaw driver, while the identity of two others could not be immediately known.

South Surma police officer-in-charge M Mursalin said the accident took place when a Habiganj-bound bus hit the CNG-run auto-rickshaw on Telibazar Bridge around 11:00am, leaving driver Salam and two passengers dead on the spot
