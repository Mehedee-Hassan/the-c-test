4 robber suspects arrested in city
Detective Branch of Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) claimed to have arrested four robber suspects in Dhaka.

DMP, in a text message sent to media on Wednesday, said that the detained were involved in looting Tk 30 lakh from Moghbazar area last month.

A press briefing will be held at DMP Media Centre at about 12:00pm in this connection.
