Man killed in police ‘gunfight’
A man was killed in an alleged gunfight with police in Sadar upazila of Magura on Sunday night.

The diseased was identified as Showkat Mandal, 38, a resident of Madhukhali upazila of Faridpur district.

Magura senior assistant police superintendent Shudarshan Ray told this to Prothom Alo.

According to the police, Showkat Mandal was a member of an inter-district robber gang.

Police also said Showkat was sued in more than one robbery cases.
