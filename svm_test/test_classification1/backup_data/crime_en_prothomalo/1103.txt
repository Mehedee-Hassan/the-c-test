Robbery suspect killed in police shootout
The suspected ringleader of a robber gang was killed in a ‘gunfight’ with police at Kualakandi in Madarganj upazila on Monday night.



The deceased was identified as Shamim Fakir, 28, son of Lal Miah Fakir of Teghoria in the upazila in Jamalpur.
