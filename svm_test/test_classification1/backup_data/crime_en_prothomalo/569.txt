Prothom Alo reporter attacked
The local correspondent of Prothom Alo was beaten by the supporters of Awami League-backed chairman candidate at Terakhada upazila in Khulna this morning.

Supporters of Sharfuddin Bishwas Bachchu beat up reporter Abdullahil Warish as he went to collect news about their vote forgery at Anandanagar Government Primary School around 9:30am.

Presiding officer Hasibur Rahman rescued the journalist with help of police.
