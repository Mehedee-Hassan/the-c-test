Chuadanga gang rape prime accused detained
Police arrested the prime accused in connection with gang rape of a girl from Bus Terminal area of Sadar upazila of Chuadanga on Sunday night.

The arrestee is Sabuj Ali, 22, son of Farhad Ali, a resident of Satgari village of the upazila.

Police said a case was filed with the Sadar police station accusing four young men including Sabuj in connection of the gang rape at the village on 12 April.

Sabuj went into hiding after the filing of the case, said Tozammel Haque, officer-in-charge of Sadar police station.

Police arrested Sabuj from the area around 10.30am, added the OC.
