Rangpur Jubo Dal leader injured in bomb blast
A Jubo Dal leader lost his fingers in an explosion allegedly while making bomb in Rangpur city on Tuesday.

The victim Firoz Sarkar, is the assistant secretary of Rangpur Jubo Dal, the police said.

Witnesses said Firoze lost his three fingers when he was trying to make bombs at the house of another BNP activist Rasel Mia in the Robertsonganj area of the city.

The police arrested him and took him to the Rangpur Medical College Hospital.

"Bomb was being made in that house. The resident of the house fled away after the explosion," the Kotwali police officer-in-charge Abdul Kader Zilani said.
