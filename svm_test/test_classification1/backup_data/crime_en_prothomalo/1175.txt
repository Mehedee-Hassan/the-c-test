Study tour buses robbed at Gangni
Four buses of Meherpur Poura College were robbed near Khalishakundi Bridge at Gangni upazila of Meherpur early Monday.

About 20-22 armed robbers stopped the buses placing logs on the road while the students-teachers of the college were returning in the buses after a study tour in Naogaon around 3:00am.

Principal Ekramul Azim said the dacoits robbed mobile phone sets, ornaments, and money from over 200 students and teachers.

Gangni police visited the crime scene, but could not arrest any criminal.
