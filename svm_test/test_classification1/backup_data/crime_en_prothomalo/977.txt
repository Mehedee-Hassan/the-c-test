Road accidents kill 4 in Ashulia
Four people were killed in separate road accidents at Ashulia on Wednesday.

The deceased are -Rafiqul Islam, 35, Abbas Ali, 55, Shafiqul Islam, 35, and one other man who could not be identified yet.

Police said, a cattle-laden pick up van collided with a truck on the Abdullahpur-Baipail road in Tangibari area of Ashulia at 4:00am, causing three fatalities.

Ashulia police sub-inspector Habibur Rahman confirmed the accident.

In a separate accident, an unidentified bicycle rider was ran over by a bus at Unique area in the same road at 5:00am.
