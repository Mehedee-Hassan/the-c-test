‘Terrorist’ held after army ‘gunfight’
Members of Bangladesh Army arrested an alleged terrorist after a gunfight with members of a terrorist group at Kamukyachara in Dighinala upazila early Monday, said a press release.

According to the Inter Services Public Relations (ISPR) press release, army arrested Nantu Chakma, 45.

The press release said the army personnel also recovered a machine gun, two SMGs, two SLRs, a grenade, nine magazines and around 350 rounds of bullets from the spot.

Seven sets of army uniform, eight mobile chargers and torchlight were also recovered from the spot.
