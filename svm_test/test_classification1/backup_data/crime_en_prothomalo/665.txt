Deputy secretary arrested for corruption
The Anti Corruption Commission (ACC) on Monday arrested a deputy secretary of the Public Administration Ministry from the city’s Maghbazar area for allegedly accumulating wealth illegally.



The arrestee was identified as Dr. Mohammad Abdul Amin, reports UNB.



An ACC team, led by its deputy director Akhter Hamid Bhuiyan, arrested Amin from the area in the morning on charge of accumulating wealth illegally.
