Home minister now says Sagar-Runi murder probe to see light
Home minister Asaduzzaman Khan Kamal on Saturday said the investigation into journalist couple Sagar Sarowar and Meherun Nahar Runi murder case will soon see the light, reports UNB.

“Our RAB members are probing Sagar-Runi murder case following the court's direction. The latest development about the case we've come to know that the DNA samples of two people have been found and efforts are on to match those. I think, it'll see the light very soon and we'll be able to unearth the motive behind the murder,” he said.

The minister came up with the remarks while talking to reporters on Arunima Resort Golf Club premises under Naragati thana in Narail.

Talking to reporters at Armed Police Battalion Headquarters two days ago, Asaduzzaman said he was unaware of progress report of the investigation into the Sagar-Runi murder case.

On 11 February 2012, Maasranga Television news editor Sagar Sarwar and ATN Bangla senior reporter Meherun Runi were stabbed to death in their bedroom in Paschim Rajabazar in the capital.

Different organisations are observing the fifth anniversary of the sensational murder case renewing their demand for trial of the killers.
