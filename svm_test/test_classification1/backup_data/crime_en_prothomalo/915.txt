2 killed in bomb blast; arms recovered
Two people were killed in a bomb explosion in Sherpur upazila of the district Sunday night.



The identities of the deceased could not be known.



Local people said, a bomb went off inside the house of Mahbubur Rahman at Juanpur Kuthibari village at about 9 pm which left one dead on the spot and another injured.



Being informed, police rushed to the spot and sent the injured to Shaheed Ziaur Rahman Medical College Hospital where he died at about 11 pm.



Superintendent of Police (SP) Asaduzzaman said a bomb disposal team this morning recovered 20 hand bombs, four foreign made pistols, 40 bullets, grenades, ammunition and bomb making materials from the house.



The team started investigation into the matter, the SP added.
