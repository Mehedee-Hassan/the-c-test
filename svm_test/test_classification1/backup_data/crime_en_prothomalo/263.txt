4 Jamaat men held in Sherpur
Police arrested four activists of Bangladesh Jamaat-e-Islami from different places of Sribordi upazila headquarters on Tuesday night for their involvement in vandalism and subversive activities.

The arrestees are Imran Hossain, 22, Abdul Jalil, 32, Abul Hossain, 26, and Kasem Ali, 31, residents of the upazila headquarters.

Sribordi police officer-in-charge (OC) Belayet Hossain Tarafder said a team of police conducted a drive in different areas of the upazila at night and arrested the four Jamaat men.

They were arrested for carrying out vandalism and arson attacks during the BNP-led 18-party-enforced nationwide hartals and blockades, the OC added.
