Washiqur murder suspects on 8-day remand
A Dhaka court on Tuesday placed the two suspects, detained over blogger Washiqur Rahman murder, on eight-day remand.

Metropolitan magistrate Younus Khan granted eight-day police remand for the two madrassa students -- Zikirullah and Ariful Islam.

Mizanur Rahman, officer-in-charge of Tejgaon industrial area police station, produced the two suspects before the court seeking 10-day remand.

Washiqur was hacked to death in city's Begunbari area on Monday morning.

Among the three murder suspects, two were caught by the local people and handed over to the police.
