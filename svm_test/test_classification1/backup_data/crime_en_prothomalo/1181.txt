4 human traffickers held with fake passports
Members of the Crime Investigation Department (CID) of police detained four human traffickers on Monday night with one thousand fake passports from Matijheel in the capital.

CID special police superintendent Abdul Kahar Akand said a number of fake visas for various countries were also seized in the drive. The CID will hold a press briefing in this regard later in the day, he added.
