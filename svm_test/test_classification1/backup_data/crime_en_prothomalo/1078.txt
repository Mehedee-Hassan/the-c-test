3 suspected robbers lynched in Barisal
Local people beat three robbery suspects to death at Baher Char village of Baro Jalia union at Hizla upazila in Barisal around 2:15 am on Monday.

Police recovered the bodies of Hanif Sardar, 25, Harun Bepari, 23, and Miraj Molla, 22, and sent them to Sher-e-Bangla Medical College and Hospital morgue.
