France condemns Dhaka attack
France has condemned the attack that took place at a Spanish restaurant in Dhaka on Friday night.

“France expresses its condolences to the families of the victims and its solidarity with Bangladesh,” France foreign ministry said in a statement on Saturday.

It said terrorism is a global scourge. “France is determined to strengthen international cooperation to combat terrorism everywhere.”
