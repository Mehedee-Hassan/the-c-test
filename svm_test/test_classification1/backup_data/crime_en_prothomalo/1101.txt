6 robbery suspects held at Uttara
Members of Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) detained six suspected robbers in the city's Uttara area on Monday morning in connection with a garment factory robbery in Gazipur.

The RAB sources said they also recovered Tk 1.17 crore from their possession and seized a truck and machinery, reports UNB news agency.

The gang looted Tk 3.41 crore breaking the vault of 'Knit Plus Garment Factory', RAB official said.

They said the details about the arrestees will be revealed at a press briefing at RAB media centre here today.
