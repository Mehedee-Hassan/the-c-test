17 held for forgery in DU entrance exam
Dhaka University (DU) authorities on Friday held 17 students from various examination centres across the Dhaka city for adopting unfair means during 'Gha' unit (Social Science Faculty) entrance examination.

Confirming the incident, DU proctor Amzad Ali said the students were detained from various examination centres for forgery in the entrance test.

The detainees, who were trying to get questions' answers over mobile phone, were caught red handed.

Now, they are being interrogated at proctor office.
