Dupchanchia BNP president arrested in Bogra
Police arrested a local leader of BNP from Eidgah area in the district town on Monday for his alleged involvement in subversive activities, including vandalism and arson attack.

The arrestee was identified as Abdul Hamid, 55, president of Dupchanchia upazila unit BNP.

Dupchanchia police officer-in-charge Nazrul Islam said arrested Hamid in the morning. The arrestee was wanted in three cases for vandalism, arson attack and subversive activities, the OC added.
