Joj Mia's mother testifies in 21 August bombing case
Jobeda Khatun, mother of 21 August Bombing accused Joj Mia, gave her testimony at the Speedy Trial Tribunal-1, temporarily set up adjacent to the Dhaka Central Jail, on Monday.

Judge Shahed Noor Uddin recorded the statement of Jobeda Khatun in the 21 August bombing case. He sets next Wednesday to quiz the defendants.

A total of 22 persons were killed and injured more than a hundred in a cluster of grenade attack on a Awami League rally in Dhaka on 21 August 2004.
