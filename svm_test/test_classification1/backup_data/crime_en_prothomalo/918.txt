2 bomb-like objects found at DMCH
Police recovered two bomb-like objects at Dhaka Medical College Hospital in the capital on Thursday morning, reports news agency UNB.

Inspector Mozammel Hossain, in-charge of DMCH police outpost, said a messenger of Dr Debesh Chandra Talukder, who is a leader of Swadhinata Chikitsak Parishad, spotted two objects, one like petrol bomb and another like crude bomb, in his room on the 1st floor of the outdoor building around 9.00am.

Later they informed this to the hospital authorities.

On information, police from the DMCH police outpost rushed to the spot.

Bomb Disposal Unit of Dhaka Metropolitan recovered the two objects to examine, added the inspector.
