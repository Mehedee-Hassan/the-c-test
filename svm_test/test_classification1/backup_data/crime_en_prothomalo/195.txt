Ex-MP Sakhawat, 11 others chargesheeted
Chargesheets were pressed against former lawmaker of a Jessore constituency Sakhawat Hossain and 11 others for their alleged involvement in the crimes against humanity in the 1971 Liberation War.

The International Crimes Tribunal-1 has set 8 September to issue an order on whether the court would accept the charges.

Five allegations including genocide, rape, arson, and looting were brought against the 12 persons.

Prosecutor Rezia Sultana, who submitted the charges to the ICT, told Prothom Alo that Sakhawat and three other accused are now in jail and the rest are absconding.
