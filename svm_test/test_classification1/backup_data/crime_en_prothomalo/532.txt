Fake DB man arrested in Ctg
The Detective Branch (DB) of Chittagong Metropolitan Police (CMP) arrested a fake detective along with foreign firearms from Chowdhury Colony at Green Valley residential area in Chittagong on Saturday afternoon.

The arrested is Ismail, 23, member of a fraud syndicate of the city.

DB additional deputy commissioner Babul Akhtar said tipped-off about the presence of a fraud syndicate at the area, police conducted a drive and arrested Ismail, who posed as a DB official, at about 5:00pm.

Police also recovered two foreign pistols, two light guns, four rounds of bullets and a wireless handset from his possession.

Police said they are trying to arrest the other members of the gang.
