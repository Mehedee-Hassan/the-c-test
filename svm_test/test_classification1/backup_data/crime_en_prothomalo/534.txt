7 Nigerians held on fraud charges
Seven Nigerians and a local man were detained by the Rapid Action Battalion in the capital city on charge of fraudulence through mobile communication.

According to an SMS from RAB, the foreigners cheated money out of people through mobile communication, alluring them of winning lottery prizes.

The details will be briefed in a press conference later today (Tuesday), the SMS reads.
