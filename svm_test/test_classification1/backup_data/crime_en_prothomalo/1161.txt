Another 7-Murder accused held
Police on Tuesday arrested Narayanganj Seven Murder accused Ruhul Amin in Shaneshwar at Bauphal in Patuakhali.

Bauphal police officer-in-charge, Naresh Chandra Karmakar, said Ruhul Amin was arrested on early Tuesday.

He was a military lance corporal. He joined RAB-11 in a deputation during the Seven Murder incident, the OC said.
