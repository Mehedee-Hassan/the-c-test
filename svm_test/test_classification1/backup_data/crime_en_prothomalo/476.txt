Azharul sentenced to death
International Crimes Tribunal-1 on Tuesday sentenced Jamaat leader ATM Azharul Islam to death for his crimes against humanity during the Liberation War in 1971.



Earlier, Azhar was taken to the International Crimes Tribunal court premises today (Tuesday) morning.



On August 23 last year, Azharul was arrested from his Moghbazar residence in the capital and produced before the tribunal. He has since been in jail.



On 12 November last year, the tribunal indicted Azhar on six specific charges of crimes against humanity.

The charges include abduction, confinement, murder, genocide, looting and arson.

A total of 19 witnesses testified against the accused.
