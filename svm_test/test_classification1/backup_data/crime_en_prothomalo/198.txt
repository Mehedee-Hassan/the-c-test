Mobarak's verdict Monday
The verdict regarding Mobarak Hossain, on trial for his crimes against humanity during Liberation War, will be delivered tomorrow (Monday).

He is a former Awami League leader of Mogra union of Akhaura upazila, Brahmanbaria.

The three-member International Crimes Tribunal 1, headed by Enayetur Rahim, announced the verdict date on Monday morning.

Earlier on June 2, the tribunal kept the verdict date on Case Awaiting Verdict (CAV).

On April 23, ICT-1 framed five charges, including killing, abduction, looting, and arson, against Mobarak.
