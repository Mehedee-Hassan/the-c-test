6 suspected abductors arrested: Police
The Anti-Kidnapping Squad of Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP), through a text message, informed media that they arrested six suspected kidnappers including a woman from Badda and Mirpur on Tuesday night.

Police will update further details about the abductors in a press briefing be held at about 12:00pm on Wednesday at DMP media centre.
