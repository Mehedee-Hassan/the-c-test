Child killed as car runs over
A child was killed as a car ran over him behind Mirpur College at Mirpur section-2 about 9:30am on Monday.

The deceased is Yasin, aged around 2 and half years.

Mirpur police sub-inspector Motiur Rahman said that the child died on the spot. Police seized the car but the driver fled the spot immediately after the accident.
