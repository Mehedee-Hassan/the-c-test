Housewife killed for dowry in Jessore
A housewife was allegedly tortured to death by her husband and in-laws over dowry at Daherpara village in Sadar upazila on Sunday.

The deceased was identified as Khadiza Begum, 22, wife of Akhter Hossain, a resident of the village.

The victim’s family members said Akhter married Khadiza three years ago. Right from the beginning of their marriage, Akhter,along with his family members used to torture Khadiza over dowry.

On Sunday noon, they started pressuring Khadiza to bring money from her family and beat her.

At one stage, they strangulated her and hung the body from the ceiling of the house to make it appear like a suicide.

On information, Khadiza’s family members rushed to the spot. They alleged that they found several injury marks on her body.

Later, the victim’s uncle Mizanur Rahman filed a case with Kotwali Police Station against Akhter and his family member in this connection.
