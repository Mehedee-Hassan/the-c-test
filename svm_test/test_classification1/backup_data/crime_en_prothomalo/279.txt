RAB arrests terrorist Nahid Serniabat
Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) on Friday arrested two alleged terrorists in possession of a foreign pistol, one magazine and two bullets in the Golapbagh area of Jatrabari in Dhaka.



Assistant Director of RAB’s legal and media wing ASP Mizanur Rahman confirmed the news saying, “Nahid Serniabat, 30, and his accomplice Jubayer Ahmed, 29, were arrested for their alleged



involvement in tender snatching, land grabbing and terrorist acts.”



“They are also accused of several criminal cases under different police stations in Barisal,” he further said.



A legal action is underway with Jatrabari police station in this connection.
