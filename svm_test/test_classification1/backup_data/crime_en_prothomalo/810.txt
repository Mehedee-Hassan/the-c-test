1 to be hanged for killing wife
A Satkhira court on Wednesday sentenced one person to death for killing his wife on April 27, 2006.

The court also fined him Tk 5,000.

The convict Siddique Gazi is son of Wahed Ali Gazi of the village of Paddashakhra under Satkhira sadar upazila of the district.

Siddique Gazi beat his wife Rikta Sultana to death as Rikta refused to meet her husband's demand of dowry of Tk 50,000 on the day.

Women and Child Repression Prevention Tribunal Judge Sahenuzzaman handed down the verdict.
