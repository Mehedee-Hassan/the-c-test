Youth held in Rangpur over gang rape
Police arrested a young man in connection with the gang-rape of two female students of Northern Nursing Institute at Bhat in the city on Tuesday night, reports news agency UNB.

The arrestee was identified as Alamgir Hossain, a 3rd year student of the institute.

Officer-in-charge of Kotwali police station ABM Zahidul Islam said the two female students of the nursing institute of Northern Medical College went to a mess in the same area on the night of 27 October to meet a friend.

However, a group of three youths, including Alamgir, violated them one after another.

The victims filed a case with Kotwali police station on Tuesday, the OC added.
