Man arrested over Faruqi murder case
Dhaka Metropolitan Police Detective Branch (DB) on Saturday arrested a man from a mosque courtyard in Mohammadpur of Dhaka, for being involved in murder of Supreme Court Mosque Khatib Nurul Islam Faruqi.

The arrestee was Sheikh Mozaffar Bin Mohsin.

Nurul Islam Faruqi was killed on 27 August at his East Rajabazar residence in Dhaka.
