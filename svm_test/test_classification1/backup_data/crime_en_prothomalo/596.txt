Comilla road crash leaves 2 dead
At least two people, including a driver of a bus, were killed and 15 others injured when a bus crashed into a roadside tree on Dhaka-Chittagong highway in Chandina upazila of Comilla on Wednesday night.

Identity of the deceased could not be known immediately.

A Chandpur-bound bus of Padma Paribahan from Dhaka hit the tree near Palki cinema of the area after the driver lost his control over the steering, leaving the driver and another person dead on the spot at around 11:45pm, said Elliotganj police.

The injured were rushed to different local hospitals and clinics.
