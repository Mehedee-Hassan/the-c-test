144 Ctg Jamaat-Shibir men charged for vandalism
Charges have been pressed against 144 leaders and activists of Jamaat-Shibir, including district Jamaat chief ANM Shamsul Islam and Shajahan Chowdhury, in a case filed over vandalising vehicles and attacking cops in 2012.

Panchlaish police sub-inspector Akram Hossain, also investigation officer of the case, submitted a charge-sheet to a local court on Tuesday.

Chittagong Metropolitan Police (CMP) assistant commissioner (prosecution) Nirmalendu Bikash Chakrabarty confirmed the matter.

On November 18, 2012, the Jamaat-Shibir activists vandalised nine vehicles including a police pickup, three shops and the campus of Premier University in Panchlaish area of the city.

They also attacked police and looted 20 bullets from the police van.
