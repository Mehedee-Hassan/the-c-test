Charges pressed against ex-MP Shakhawat,11 others
Charges have been pressed against Jessore’s former parliament member Shakhawat Hossain and 11 others for their alleged war crimes.

International Crime Tribunal-1 fixed 8 September to decide about acceptance of the charges.

The accused have been charged with murder, rape, arsoning, and looting.

Public prosecutor Razia Sultana told Prothom Alo four of 12 accused are in prison and arrest warrant has been issued for eight others.
