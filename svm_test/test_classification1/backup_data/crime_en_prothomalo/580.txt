3 DMDs, GM of Basic Bank removed
State-owned Basic Bank has removed its three deputy managing directors and a general manager for their alleged forgery.

The action was taken at a meeting of the board of directors of the bank on Tuesday.

The sacked DMDs - Fazlus Sobhan, Ruhul Alam and Mohammad Selim - and the GM - Mahbubul Alam - were served notices in this connection on Wednesday.

There are loan forgery cases against all of them excepting Ruhul Alam.
