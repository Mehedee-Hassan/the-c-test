Hassan charged with six offences
Investigators have finalised their report on Syed Hassan Ali of Tarail upazila in Kishoreganj over his involvement in crimes against humanity during the Liberation War.

He has been charged with six types of offences, including murder, genocide, arson attacks and other crimes against humanity.

It has been confirmed at a press conference in the Safe Home office of the investigation wing of the International Crimes Tribunal at Dhanmodi in Dhaka on Sunday.

Investigation coordinator Abdul Hannan Khan and M Sanaul Haque were present at the press conference.

They said the investigation report against Hassan Ali will be submitted to the prosecution lawyer's office today (Monday).

International Crimes Tribunal issued arrest warrant against Hassan Ali but he has not been arrested yet.
