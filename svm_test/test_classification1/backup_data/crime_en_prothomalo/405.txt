19 sentenced to life in two districts
Separate courts in Noakhali and Mymensing sentenced 19 people to life imprisonment for murder, abduction and kidnapping cases on Wednesday.

In Noakhali, the session judges court sentenced six people to life term imprisonment in separate cases.

In Mymensing, special judge court sentenced 13 people to life imprisonment for murder of five members of a family, reported news agency UNB.
