Dhaka, Washington agree to combat terrorism jointly
The United States (US) has agreed to work with Bangladesh police to combat terrorism in the country and it (US) is keen to provide assistance to police force for increasing their efficiency.

The decision was reached at a meeting when a five-member US delegation led by diplomatic security assistance secretary Gregory Starr called on inspector general of police (IGP) AKM Shahidul Haq at police headquarters in Dhaka on Wednesday.

US ambassador in Dhaka Marcia Bernicat accompanied the delegation during the meeting.

Among others, DIG (operation) lawyer Mahbubur Rahman and DIG (crime) M Humayun Kabir were also present.
