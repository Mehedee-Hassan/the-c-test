DB team to visit US
A three-member team of detective branch (DB) of police will soon tour the United States as part of its investigation into the alleged plot to abduct and kill prime minister Sheikh Hasina’s son Sajeeb Wazed Joy.



The team will be headed by deputy commissioner of DB Mashruqur Rahman Khaled.



Another two members are additional deputy commissioner Razib Al Masud and senior assistant commissioner Hasan Arafat.



Additional commissioner of Dhaka Metropolitan Police, Monirul Islam told this at a press briefing on Tuesday.



He said police searched Shafik Rehman’s house to seize some important documents.

He, however, did not see exactly when the investigation team will leave for the US.
