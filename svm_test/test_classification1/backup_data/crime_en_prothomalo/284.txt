‘Terrorist’ held after ‘gunfight’ with army
Members of the Bangladesh army arrested an alleged terrorist after a gunfight with members of a terrorist group at Kamukyachara in Dighinala upazila early Monday.

Identity of the arrestee could not be known immediately.

Sources said the army men also recovered one SMG, two SLR, six magazines and 139 rounds of bullets from the spot.

The details will be revealed in a briefing later.
