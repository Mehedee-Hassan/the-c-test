Robbers kill college girl in Brahmanbaria
A college girl was killed by robbers during a robbery incident at Noagaon village in Sarail upazila in Brahmanbaria early Saturday.

The deceased is Ratna, 18, a first year student at Islampur Kazi Shafiqul Islam College.

Local people said a gang of robbers stormed the house of Osman Mia and kept the house inmates hostage at gunpoint in the dead of night.

Later, the robbers looted valuables including gold ornaments and cash from the house.

The robbers also hit Ratna and three other house inmates with sharp weapons as they screamed for help, leaving Ratna dead on the spot and others injured.
