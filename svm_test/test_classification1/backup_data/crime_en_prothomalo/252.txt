Mirsarai upazila chairman of BNP sent to jail
A Chittagong court sent Mirsari Upazila Chairman and Member Secretary of upazila BNP Nurul Amin to jail after rejecting his bail prayer on Sunday.

Chittagong district and session judge S M Nurul Huda sent him to jail after his surrender before the court for bail.

Nurul Amin was wanted in 15 cases for acts of sabotage and vandalism at different police stations, court sources said.
