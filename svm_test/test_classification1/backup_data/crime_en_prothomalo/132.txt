Child among 3 die in landslide
A 3-member family was buried in a landslide triggered by rainfall at Shahbazpur tea garden in Baralekha of Moulvibazar on Friday night.

The deceased are tea labourer Rajesh, 35, his wife Manju, 30, and their one-year-old child.

Baralekha police inspector (investigation) Akbar Hossain said the bodies were recovered in early hours of Saturday.

Police said the family members were killed as a hillside collapsed onto their house and they were crushed under it.
