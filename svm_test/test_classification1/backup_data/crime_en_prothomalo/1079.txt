9 'militants' held in city
The Detective Branch (DB) of police arrested nine suspected militants of banned Islamist group Harkatul Jihad Bangladesh (HuJi B) and Ansarullah Bangla Team from Dhaka on Sunday.

In a mobile text message sent to the media, police demanded that the arrested men were planning a bank robbery.

Details of the arrest will be disclosed at a press conference at Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) media centre at 11:30am, it further said.
