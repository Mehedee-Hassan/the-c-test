'Leguna' driver stabbed to death in Pallabi
Miscreants stabbed the driver of a Leguna, utility vehicle used for public transport, to death in early hours of Sunday.

The deceased is Md Hassan, 20, a resident of Pallabi area.

No case has been filed in this connection since 9:00am on Sunday.

Pallabi police officer-in-charge Syed Ziauzzaman said miscreants stabbed Hassan near Pallabi bus stand at about 4:00am and fled the spot.

Later, local people took Hassan to Dhaka Medical College Hospital in critical condition where the emergency unit doctors declared his death.

The reasons behind the incident could not be known immediately.
