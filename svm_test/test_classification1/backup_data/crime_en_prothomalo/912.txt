Crude bomb hurled at bus in Comilla
A group of alleged saboteurs hurled a petrol bomb at a passenger bus in Chandina upazila of Comilla on Saturday night.

However, no injury was reported.

Witness said some unidentified miscreants hurled the petrol bomb at the Chittagong-bound bus of 'Unique Paribahan' at Chandina Sadar area at 11:15pm.

Chandina police officer-in-charge Golam Morshed confirmed the incident.

Source: UNB, Dhaka
