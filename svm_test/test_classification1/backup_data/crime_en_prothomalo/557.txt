Foreigner, 3 bankers remanded for ATM forgery
German national Thomas Peter and three officials of City Bank have been remanded in police custody for their alleged involvement in the recent fraud in ATM booths of a few banks.

Dhaka metropolitan magistrate Mazharul Islam on Monday put them on 6-day remand each.

The foreigner was picked up from Dhaka city’s Gulshan area on Sunday night. The bank officials detained are Morshed Alam Maksud, Rezaul Karim and Reaz Ahmed.

Swindlers drew cash from four booths of three private commercial banks in Dhaka recently.
