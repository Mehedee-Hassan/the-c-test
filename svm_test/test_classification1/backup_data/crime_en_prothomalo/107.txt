3 children die in Asian Hospital
Three children died in Asian Cardiac and General Hospital at Lalmatia in Dhaka on Friday night.

No doctors were available on Saturday at the hospital.

Panicked parents are shifting their children admitted at the hospital.

The children died within 9:00pm to 12:00am on Friday.

No doctors were seen in the morning. Only a nurse were present at the ICU where a child is under treatment

Ehsanuddin, a relative of the child, said they are preparing to shift the child as they heard six children died in last four days in this hospital.

Currently, three children are under treatment at the hospital.

Police has been deployed at the hospital.

Kamruzzaman, sub-inspector of Mohammadpur Police station confirmed the deaths
