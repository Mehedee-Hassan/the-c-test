Bagerhat ‘JCD man’ killed in bomb blast
A suspected activist of Jatiyatabadi Chhatra Dal was killed in a bomb blast at Attaki Ghoshpara in Fakirhat upazila on Monday.



Bazlur Rashid, officer-in-charge of Fakirhat Police station, said Sumon Sheikh, 22, a Chhatra Dal activist, found a hand bomb in a pile of straw at his house in the afternoon.



When he touched the bomb it went off, leaving him critically injured.

Later, Sumon died on way to Khulna Medical College Hospital.



On information, police rushed in and recovered some samples of the exploded bomb. They detained Sumon’s mother Parul Begum for interrogation.
