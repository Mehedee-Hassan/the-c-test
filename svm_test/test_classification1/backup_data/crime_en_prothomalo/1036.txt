Tutor held on rape charge
Three people, including a private tutor, were detained on charge of raping a schoolgirl in Dhaka on Wednesday night.

The victim was taken to Dhaka Medical College Hospital (DMCH) on Thursday morning.

The DMCH sources said policemen of Mirpur police station brought the schoolgirl to the hospital. She is now undergoing treatment, the sources added.

One of the relatives of the girl said the house tutor called her to his home and raped her on Wednesday.

Later, on an allegation filed by the victim family, police held the tutor and his parents.
