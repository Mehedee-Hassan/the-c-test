One killed, 5 injured in bomb blasts
One person died and five others sustained injuries in bomb blasts in different parts of the country on Wednesday night.

Mohammad Ridwan, 52, was killed in a crude bomb explosion in Kalurghat area of Chittagong, local sources said.

Five other people were injured as miscreants exploded petrol bombs in Gulshan and Lalbagh areas in Dhaka city.
